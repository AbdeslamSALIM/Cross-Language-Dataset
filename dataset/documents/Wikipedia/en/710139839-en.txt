Real Murcia Club de Fútbol, S.A.D. is a Spanish football club based in Murcia, they were founded in 1908.The team finished 19th in La Liga last season and was relegated to Segunda División. Murcia's home stadium is La Nueva Condomina which can hold 33,045 spectators.HistoryReal Murcia founded in 1908 is a football club based in the city of Murcia, The team was promoted to La Liga on May 11th, 2007. By next spring, the team had been relegated to the Segunda División again. Its home stadium is La Nueva Condomina with a capacity of 33,078.
