Escaldes-Engordany is one of the parishes of Andorra. The parish is composed of the villages Escaldes, Engordany, Vilars d'Engordany, Engolasters, and El Fener. As of 2005 it has a population of 16,918. Notable events include the town's annual jazz festival.
