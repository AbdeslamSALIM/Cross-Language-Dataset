 In Greek mythology, Menoetius (Greek Menoitios) referred to several different people.A son of Iapetus and Clymene. A glorious warrior who was insolent to Zeus. By some accounts he was smitten by Zeus with a lightning bolt on Mount Triphyle, and in others he was merely crippled and banished to Tartarus. This Titan's name means "ruined strength".One of Hades' shepherds on Erythea.
