In 2003, he moved to Quick Step-Davitamon, and the following year to French squad Crédit Agricole.After two seasons in that team, Kashechkin joined Team Astana, where he posted good results throughout the 2006 season.Kashechkin was in 8th place in the overall classification after 15 stages of the 2007 Tour de France. However, his Tour was ended when his Astana team withdrew from the event after team leader Alexander Vinokourov tested positive for blood doping.Following the 2007 Tour de France, Kashechkin has tested positive for blood doping.
