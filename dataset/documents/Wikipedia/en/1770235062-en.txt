Bar-sur-Seine is a commune in the Aube department in north-central France.Situated on the River Seine, it is a rather quiet, pleasant, but run-down town just off the A5 autoroute. The town is famous for its castle, the Château de Bar sur Seine.See alsoCommunes of the Aube departmentReferencesBased on the article in the French Wikipedia.
