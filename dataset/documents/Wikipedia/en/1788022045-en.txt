Belgorod Oblast (, Belgorodskaya oblast) is a federal subject of Russia (an oblast). Its administrative center is the city of Belgorod. Population: 1,511,620 (2002 Census; 1,380,723 (1989 Census).Time zoneBelgorod Oblast is located in the Moscow Time Zone (MSK/MSD). UTC offset is +0300&nbsp;(MSK)/+0400&nbsp;(MSD).Administrative divisions Belgorod Oblast is administratively divided into 21 districts (raions), which are further divided into 335 rural okrugs.
