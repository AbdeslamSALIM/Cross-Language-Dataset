          Olga Nikolayevna Kaniskina (; born January 19, 1985) is a Russian race walker.She won the silver medal in the 20 km race at the 2006 European Championships and gold medals at the 2007 World Championships and the 2008 Beijing Olympics.2008 saw Olga Kaniskina break her first world record in the 20 km Walk at the Russian Championships. She lowered the mark set by her compatriot Olimpiada Ivanova in August 2005.
