 Lithuania |champion=FBK Kaunas The A Lyga (English: The A League) is the top division of professional football in Lithuania. It is organized by NFKA (Lithuanian: Nacionalinė futbolo klubų asociacija, English: National Football Club Association). There are 8 teams in the competition.
