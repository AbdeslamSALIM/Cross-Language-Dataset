 The Province of Ascoli Piceno () is a province in the Marche region of Italy. Its capital is the city of Ascoli Piceno.It has an area of 2,088 km², and a total population of 369,579 (2001). There are 73 comuni in the provincehttp://www.upinet.it/indicatore.asp?id_statistiche=6, see Comunes of the Province of Ascoli Piceno.The planned new Province of Fermo is being created from part of the province of Ascoli Piceno.
