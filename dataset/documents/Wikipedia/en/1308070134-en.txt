 Imbabura is a province in Ecuador. The capital is Ibarra. The people speak Imbabura Quechua language.The province is divided in 6 cantons.Canton (Capital)Antonio Ante (Atuntaqui)Cotacachi (Cotacachi)Ibarra (Ibarra)Otavalo (Otavalo)Pimampiro (Pimampiro)San Miguel de Urcuquí (Urcuquí)Imbabura Volcano is located in the province. It is 4609m high and best reached from the town La Esperanza.
