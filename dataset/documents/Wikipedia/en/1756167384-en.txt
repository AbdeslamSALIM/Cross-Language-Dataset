 The flag of Azerbaijan () is one of the national symbols of Azerbaijan and consists of three equal horizontal bands, from top: blue, red, and green; a white crescent and an eight-pointed star (Rub El Hizb) are centered in the red band. The eight points of the star stand for the branches? of the Turkic peoples . The blue band is the colour of the Turks. the green is for Islam and red is for progress and EuropeanisationComplete Flags of The World, 1997, page 172, Dorling Kindersley.
