Breitenbach-Haut-Rhin is a French commune in the département of the Haut-Rhin in the Alsace region of eastern France. Its inhabitants are Breitenbachois and Breitenbachoises. Its official name distinguishes it from Breitenbach, Bas-Rhin.The place name appears in thirteenth-century documents, with the significance of breit ("wide"), bach ("stream").
