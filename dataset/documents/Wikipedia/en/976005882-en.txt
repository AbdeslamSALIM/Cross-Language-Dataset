In the 1996-97 season he was named to the Big 12 Conference First Team and the Basketball Times All-American First Team.Early NBA yearsDrafted third overall in the 1997 NBA Draft by the Boston Celtics, Billups was traded to the Toronto Raptors in exchange for Kenny Anderson midway through his first season. Billups replaced the often-injured Brandon and had a breakthrough 2001-2002 season. He helped lead the Pistons to the 2004 NBA Finals, averaging 21 points and 5.2 assists per game in the series to earn the Finals MVP Award in a 4-1 victory over the Los Angeles Lakers. In 2005, the Pistons lost the Finals in seven games to the San Antonio Spurs.
