Franklin County is a county of the U.S. state of Alabama. Its name is in honor of Benjamin Franklin, famous statesman, scientist, and printer. As of the 2006 census estimates, the population was 30,847. Its county seat is Russellville and it is a dry county.HistoryFranklin County was established on February 6, 1818.Colbert CountyColbert County was originally established on February 6, 1867 after it split from Franklin County over political issues after the American Civil War.
