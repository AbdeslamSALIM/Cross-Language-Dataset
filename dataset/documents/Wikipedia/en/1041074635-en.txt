  Argun () is the Russian name of the river which is a part of the Russia&ndash;China border. Its upper reaches are known as Hailar He (海拉尔河, Hailar River) in China. Its length is 1,007&nbsp;mi (1,620&nbsp;km).The river flows from the Western slope of the Greater Khingan Range in Inner Mongolia. Its confluence with Shilka River forms the Amur River.
