 Nuoro (; Sardinian: Provìntzia de Nùgoro) is a province in the autonomous island region of Sardinia in Italy. Its capital is the city of Nuoro.It has an area of 3,934&nbsp;km², and a total population of 164,260 (2001 census). There are 52 comuni in the province, the largest of which are Nuoro (population: 36,678), Macomer (11,116), and Siniscola (10,954).
