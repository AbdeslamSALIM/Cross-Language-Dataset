| website                 = http://www.comune.savigliano.cn.it Savigliano is a commune of Piedmont, Italy, in the Province of Cuneo, 32 miles south of Turin by rail, 1053 feet above sea-level. It has a population of 19,893.It has important ironworks, foundries, locomotive works and silk manufactures, as well as sugar factories, printing works and cocoon-raising establishments. It retains some traces of its ancient walls, demolished in 1707, and has a fine collegiate church (S.
