The municipality comprises the villages of Beselare, Geluveld, Passendale, Zandvoorde and Zonnebeke proper. On January 1 2006 Zonnebeke had a total population of 11,758. The total area is 67.57 km² which gives a population density of 174 inhabitants per km².TownsThe municipality comprises the villages of Zonnebeke proper, Beselare, Geluveld, Passendale and Zandvoorde.
