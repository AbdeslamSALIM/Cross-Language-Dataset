Gila County is a county in the central part of the U.S. state of Arizona. As of the 2000 census its population was 51,335. The county seat is Globe.Gila County contains parts of Fort Apache Indian Reservation and San Carlos Indian Reservation.HistoryThe county was formed from parts of Maricopa County and Pinal County on February 8 1881. The boundary was then extended eastward to the San Carlos River by public petition in 1889.
