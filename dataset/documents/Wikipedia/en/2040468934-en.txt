Conches-en-Ouches is a commune in the Eure department in northern France. Population 5,352 (INSEE, 1999).It is located by the Rouloir river, southwest of Évreux in the Haute-Normandie region. The town is located on a plateau known as the Pays d'Ouche.See alsoCommunes of the Eure departmentReferencesL'Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques, (INSEE Census, 1999).
