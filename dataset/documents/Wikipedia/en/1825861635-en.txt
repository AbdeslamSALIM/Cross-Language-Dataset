 Lower Bavaria () is one of the seven administrative regions of Bavaria, Germany, located in the east of the state. It is subdivided into three regions ()—Landshut, Passau and Donau-Wald. Recent election results mark it as the most conservative part of Germany, generally giving huge margins to the CSU. In this part of Bavaria, there is the region Bavaria-forest.
