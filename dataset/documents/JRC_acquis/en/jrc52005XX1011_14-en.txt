Notification by the Federal Republic of Germany concerning visa reciprocity [1] 
(2005/C 251/14) 
Sir, 
In accordance with Article 2 of Council Regulation (EC) No 851/2005 of 2 June 2005 amending Regulation (EC) No 539/2001, I would inform you that, as at 24 June 2005, nationals of the Federal Republic of Germany are subject to visa requirements by third countries listed in Annex II to Regulation (EC) No 539/2001 in the following cases: 
- Australia; 
- Brunei Darussalam (for a stay of more than 30 days); 
- Venezuela (for entry by land or sea). 
A similar letter has been sent to Commission Vice-President Franco Frattini. 
[1] This notification is published in conformity with Article 2 of Council Regulation (EC) No 851/2005 of 2 June 2005 (OJ L 141, 4.6.2005, p. 3) amending Regulation (EC) No 539/2001 of 15 March 2001 (OJ L 81, 21.3.2001, p. 1).
