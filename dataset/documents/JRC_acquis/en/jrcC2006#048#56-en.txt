Case T-198/04: Judgment of the Court of First Instance of 8 December 2005 — Merladet v Commission (Officials — Career development report — 2001/2002 appraisal exercise — Proper conduct of the reporting procedure — Action for annulment) 
Judgment of the Court of First Instance of 8 December 2005 — Merladet 
v Commission 
(Case T-198/04) [1] 
Parties 
Applicant: José Félix Merladet (Overijse, Belgium) (represented by: N. Lhoëst and E. de Schietere de Lophem, lawyers) 
Defendant: Commission of the European Communities (represented by: J. Currall and H. Kraemer, Agents) 
Application for 
Annulment of the decision adopting the applicant's career development report for the 2001/2002 appraisal exercise 
Operative part of the judgment 
The Court: 
1. Annuls the decision adopting the applicant's career development report for the 2001/2002 appraisal exercise; 
2. Orders the Commission to pay the costs. 
[1] OJ C 262, 23.10.2004.
