Judgment of the Court of First Instance of 17 January 2006 — Henkel v OHIM 
(Case T-398/04) [1] 
Parties: 
Applicant: Henkel KGaA (Dusseldorf, Germany) (represented by: C. Osterrieth, lawyer)
