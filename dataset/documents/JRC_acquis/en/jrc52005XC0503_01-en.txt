Member State: France. 
Aid No: NN 160/2003 (ex N 344/2003). 
Title: Fodder aid for farmers affected by drought. 
Objective: Aid paid to herbivore farmers to compensate for losses caused by drought in 53 French departments. 
Legal basis: Non-statutory. 
Budget: EUR 50 million. 
Aid intensity or amount: The aid will be granted up to a maximum of EUR 45 per tonne of feed purchased. 
Duration: Until the budget is exhausted for feed ordered between 1 June 2003 and 31 December 2003.

Member State: Austria. 
No of the aid: N 23/2004. 
Title: Financial aid for to agricultural enterprises having sustained damage due to drought in 2003. 
Objective: 
To compensate farmers for the drought damage during 2003. In order to compensate for the drought-related damage to farms Austria proposes to grant the aid in form of an interest subsidy on bank loans taken by farmers between 1.6.2003 and 16.2.2004 in order to offset drought-related losses. The scope of the aid measure is limited to drought related production losses of at least 30 %. Insurance compensation has to be taken into account in order to exclude overcompensation. 
This aid is payable only in the following areas: Burgenland, Lower Austria, Upper Austria Styria, Carinthia, Salzburg, Vorarlberg and Wien. 
Legal basis: Sonderrichtlinie des Bundesministers für Land- und Forstwirtschaft, Umwelt und Wasserwirtschaft (BMLFUW) für Maßnahmen zur Existenzsicherung für durch Dürre 2003 geschädigte Landwirtschaftliche Gebiete 
Budget: Available resources at the time of the notification: EUR 8,0 million. Actual expenses are not yet foreseeable. 
Type of culture | Maximum loan EUR/ha | Maximum aid EUR/ha | 
Grassland | 350 | 20,86 | 
Forage | 350 | 20,86 | 
Cereals (including corn and millet) | 350 | 20,86 | 
Oil plants, protein plants, poppy | 350 | 20,86 | 
Potatoes | 1200 | 71,52 | 
Field vegetables and oil pumpkin plant | 2000 | 119,4 | 
Saatmais, elderberry | 2000 | 119,4 | 
Fruit cultures | 2500 | 149 | 
Aquaculture | 600 | 35,76 | 
Duration: One-off measure.
