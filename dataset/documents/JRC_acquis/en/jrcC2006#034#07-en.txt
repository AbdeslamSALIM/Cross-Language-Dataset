Appointment of the Registrar 
(2006/C 34/07) 
The term of office of Mr Roger Grass, Registrar of the Court of Justice of the European Communities, expired on 9 February 2006. 
The Court of Justice, at its General Meeting on 28 June 2005, decided to renew the term of office of Mr Roger Grass in accordance with Article 12 of the Rules of Procedure for the period from 10 February 2006 to 9 February 2012 inclusive.
