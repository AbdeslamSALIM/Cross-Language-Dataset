Council Decision 
of 29 November 2004 
appointing a member of the Advisory Committee of the Euratom Supply Agency 
(2004/C 305/02) 
THE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION, 
Having regard to the Treaty establishing the European Atomic Energy Community, and in particular the second and third paragraphs of Article 54 thereof, 
Having regard to Article X of the Statutes of the Euratom Supply Agency [1], as last amended by Decision 95/1/EC, Euratom, ECSC of 1 January 1995 [2], 
Having regard to the Council Decision of 22 September 2003 appointing the members of the Advisory Committee of the Euratom Supply Agency [3], 
Having regard to the opinion of the Commission, 
Whereas: 
(1) A member's seat on the above Committee has fallen vacant following the resignation of Mr Serge GAS, of which the Council was informed on 19 May 2004. 
(2) That vacancy should therefore be filled. 
(3) The French Government submitted a nomination on 19 May 2004, 
HAS DECIDED AS FOLLOWS: 
Sole Article 
Ms Jeanne MARCUCCI is hereby appointed a member of the Advisory Committee of the Euratom Supply Agency for the remainder of the Committee's term of office, ending on 28 March 2005. 
Done at Brussels, 29 November 2004. 
For the Council 
The President 
L. J. Brinkhorst 
[1] OJ 27, 6.12.1958, p. 534/58. 
[2] OJ L 1, 1.1.1995, p. 1. 
[3] OJ C 232, 27.9.2003, p. 1.
