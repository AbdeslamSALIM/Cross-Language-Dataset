Case number: NN 161/2003 
Title: Prolongation and modification of Waste Tax scheme 
Objective: To grant tax relief to some specific waste facilities and specific waste categories, for environmental reasons and in order to avoid unreasonable economic consequences to the industries concerned 
Legal basis: Lag (1999:673) om skatt på avfall 
Budget: Loss of tax revenue: SEK 580 million (ca. EUR 64 million) consisting of ca. SEK 180 million (ca. EUR 20 million) for waste which is not the direct result of the production process (for example, used products, goods, package) and ca SEK 400 million (ca. EUR 44 million) for waste which is the direct result of the production of a company 
Intensity or amount: SEK 370 (ca. EUR 40.7) per ton 
Duration: Unlimited
