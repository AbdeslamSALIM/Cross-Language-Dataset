Case T-241/03: Order of the Court of First Instance of 17 May 2006 — Marcuccio v Commission (Officials — Reassignment — Accommodation provided by employer — Decision to remove the applicant's personal effects — Action for annulment — Action for damages — Act adversely affecting an official — Inadmissibility) 
Order of the Court of First Instance of 17 May 2006 — Marcuccio 
v Commission 
(Case T-241/03) [1] 
Parties 
Applicant: Luigi Marcuccio (Tricase, Italy) (represented by: L. Garofalo, lawyer) 
Defendant: Commission of the European Communities (represented by: E. de March and C. Berardis-Kayser, acting as Agents, assisted by A. Dal Ferro, lawyer) 
Re: 
First, an application for annulment of the note of 15 October 2002 concerning the removal of the applicant's personal effects from his former accommodation provided by his employer in Angola and, second, an application for damages for the harm allegedly caused the applicant by the contents of that note 
Operative part of the order 
1. The action is dismissed as inadmissible; 
2. The applicant is to bear his own costs and pay those of the Commission. 
[1] OJ C 200, 23.8.2003.
