Authorisation for State aid pursuant to Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty 
Cases where the Commission raises no objections 
(2005/C 97/03) 
(Text with EEA relevance) 
Date of adoption of the decision: 16 November 2004 
Member State: United Kingdom, East Midlands 
Aid No: N 199/2004 
Title: Broadband Business Fund 
Objective: To support the provision of affordable broadband telecommunications services to SMEs and residential users in areas of Northamptonshire, Leicestershire, Derbyshire, Rutland, South Lincolnshire and Nottinghamshire which do not currently enjoy affordable standard broadband availability. 
Legal basis: Section 5(1) of the Regional Development Agency Act 1998, which sets out the purposes, functions and powers of the East Midlands Development Agency. 
Overall budget: Unknown, depending on offers providers 
Aid amount: Maximum GBP 1,9 million from UK State resources (expected to be reduced through a reverse payment mechanism) 
Duration of project: At least 3 years 
Other information: The provider selected to provide the service is mandated to provide non-discriminatory wholesale access to third party providers.
