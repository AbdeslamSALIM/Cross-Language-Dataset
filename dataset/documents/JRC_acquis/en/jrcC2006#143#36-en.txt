Case C-482/04 P: Order of the Court of 21 November 2005 — SNF SA v Commission of the European Communities (Appeal — Directive on cosmetic products — Restrictions on the use of polyacrylamides in the composition of cosmetic products) 
Order of the Court of 21 November 2005 — SNF SA 
v Commission of the European Communities 
(Case C-482/04 P) [1] 
Parties 
Applicant: SNF SA (represented by: K. Van Maldegem and C. Mereu, lawyers) 
Other party to the proceedings: Commission of the European Communities (represented by: X. Lewis and A. Caeiros, acting as Agents) 
Re: 
Appeal against the Order of the Court of First Instance (Fifth Chamber) of 6 September 2004 in Case T-213/02 SNF v Commission, declaring inadmissible an application for the partial annulment of the Twenty-sixth Commission Directive 2002/34/EC of 15 April 2002 adapting to technical progress Annexes II, III and VII to Council Directive 76/748/EEC on the approximation of the laws of the Member States relating to cosmetic products (OJ 2002 L 102, p. 19) 
Operative part of the order 
1. The appeal is dismissed. 
2. SNF SA is ordered to pay the costs. 
[1] OJ C 19, 22.1.2005.
