Decision of the EEA Joint Committee No 90/2003 of 11 July 2003 amending Annex II (Technical regulations, standards, testing and certification) to the EEA Agreement 
Decision of the EEA Joint Committee 
No 90/2003 
of 11 July 2003 
amending Annex II (Technical regulations, standards, testing and certification) to the EEA Agreement 
THE EEA JOINT COMMITTEE, 
Having regard to the Agreement on the European Economic Area, as amended by the Protocol adjusting the Agreement on the European Economic Area, hereinafter referred to as %quot%the Agreement%quot%, and in particular Article 98 thereof, 
Whereas: 
(1) Annex II to the Agreement was amended by Decision of the EEA Joint Committee No 129/2002 of 27 September 2002(1). 
(2) Directive 2000/70/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 16 November 2000 amending Council Directive 93/42/EEC as regards medical devices incorporating stable derivates of human blood or human plasma(2) is to be incorporated into the Agreement, 
HAS DECIDED AS FOLLOWS: 
Article 1 
The following indent shall be added in point 1 (Council Directive 93/42/EEC) of Chapter XXX of Annex II to the Agreement: 
%quot%- 32000 L 0070: Directive 2000/70/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 16 November 2000 (OJ L 313, 13.12.2000, p. 22).%quot% 
Article 2 
The texts of Directive 2000/70/EC in the Icelandic and Norwegian languages, to be published in the EEA Supplement to the Official Journal of the European Union, shall be authentic. 
Article 3 
This Decision shall enter into force on 12 July 2003, provided that all the notifications in Article 103(1) of the Agreement have been made to the EEA Joint Committee(3). 
Article 4 
This Decision shall be published in the EEA section of, and in the EEA Supplement to, the Official Journal of the European Union. 
Done at Brussels, 11 July 2003. 
For the EEA Joint Committee 
The President 
H. S. H. Prinz Nikolaus von Liechtenstein 
(1) OJ L 336, 12.12.2002, p. 29. 
(2) OJ L 313, 13.12.2000, p. 22. 
(3) No constitutional requirements indicated.
