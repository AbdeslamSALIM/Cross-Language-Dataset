Order of the Court (Third Chamber) of 13 October 2005 in Case C-1/05 SA: Intek Co. v Commission of the European Communities (Application for authorisation to serve a garnishee order on the Commission of the European Communities) 
Order of the Court 
(Third Chamber) 
of 13 October 2005 
in Case C-1/05 SA: Intek Co. 
v Commission of the European Communities [1] 
In Case C-1/05 SA: application for authorisation to serve a garnishee order on the Commission of the European Communities brought on 28 January 2005, Intek Co., established in Ashgabat (Turkmenistan) (avocat: R. Nathan) against Commission of the European Communities (Agents: J-F. Pasquier and E. Manhaeve) — the Court (Third Chamber), composed of A. Rosas, President of the Chamber, J. Malenovský (Rapporteur), A. La Pergola, J.-P. Puissochet and A. Ó Caoimh, Judges; C. Stix-Hackl, Advocate General; R. Grass, Registrar, made an order on 13 October 2005, the operative part of which is as follows 
1. There is no need to adjudicate. 
2. Each party shall bear its own costs. 
[1] OJ C 82 of 2.4.2005
