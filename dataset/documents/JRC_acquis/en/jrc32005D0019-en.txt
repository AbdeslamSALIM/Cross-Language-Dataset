2005/19/EC, Euratom: Council Decision of 22 December 2004 appointing an Austrian member of the Economic and Social Committee 
Council Decision 
of 22 December 2004 
appointing an Austrian member of the Economic and Social Committee 
(2005/19/EC, Euratom) 
THE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION, 
Having regard to the Treaty establishing the European Community, and in particular Article 259 thereof, 
Having regard to the Treaty establishing the European Atomic Energy Community, and in particular Article 167 thereof, 
Having regard to the Council Decision 2002/758/EC, Euratom of 17 September 2002 appointing the members of the Economic and Social Committee for the period from 21 September 2002 to 20 September 2006 [1], 
Having regard to the nomination submitted by the Austrian Government, 
Having obtained the opinion of the Commission of the European Union, 
HAS DECIDED AS FOLLOWS: 
Sole Article 
Mr Hans KLETZMAYR is hereby appointed a member of the Economic and Social Committee in place of Mr Rudolf STRASSER for the remainder of the latter's term of office, which runs until 20 September 2006. 
Done at Brussels, 22 December 2004. 
For the Council 
The President 
C. Veerman 
-------------------------------------------------- 
[1] OJ L 253, 21.9.2002, p. 9.
