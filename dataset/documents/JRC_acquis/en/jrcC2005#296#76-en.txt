Communication 
(2005/C 296/76) 
On 9 November 2005, Waltraud Hakenberg was appointed Registrar of the European Union Civil Service Tribunal for a term of six years, in accordance with Article 3(4) of Council Decision 2004/752/EC, Euratom of 2 November 2004 establishing the European Union Civil Service Tribunal, and Articles 20 and 7 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of First Instance.
