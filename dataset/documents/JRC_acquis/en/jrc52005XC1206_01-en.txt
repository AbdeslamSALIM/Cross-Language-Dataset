Title : Assistance in agricultural areas affected by natural disasters (torrential rain in the province of Cagliari on 3, 4 and 5 April 2005)

Member State : Italy (Umbria) 
Aid No : N 308/2004 
Title : Amendments to Law No 20 of 27 November 2003 Interventions in favour of livestock farmers taking part in the bluetongue vaccination plan 
Objective : The measures aim to compensate livestock breeders for extra costs and losses suffered as a result of implementing the bluetongue vaccination plan in Region Umbria 
Legal basis : Modificazioni ed integrazioni alla legge 27 novembre 2003 n. 20 — Interventi a favore degli allevatori partecipanti al piano vaccinale per la febbre catarrale degli ovini (blue-tongue) 
Budget : EUR 150000/year 
Aid intensity or amount : 
Costs related to the ban on animal movements: 100 % 
Losses due to reduced natality: 90 % (–10 % franchise) 
Duration : Unlimited 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption of the decision : 30.8.2005 
Member State : Italy (Veneto) 
Aid No : N 326/2005 
Title : Assistance aimed at increasing the value of milk products 
Objective : Boosting the quality of milk products — promotion and advertising of milk products 
Legal basis : Legge regionale 25 febbraio 2005, n. 9, art. 4: "Interventi per la promozione e la valorizzazione delle produzioni lattiero-casearie" 
Budget : EUR 1570000 for the first year and, subsequently, a budget set in the annual Finance Acts 
Aid intensity or amount : 
Promotion and boosting of quality: 75 %, with a ceiling of EUR 100000 per beneficiary per three-year period (or 50 % in the case of SMEs, with the higher equivalent amount being applied to the latter) 
Advertising: 50 % 
Duration : Six years

Member State : Portugal 
Aid No : N 375/2005 
Title : Compensation for damage caused by drought. Credit line for potato and citrus fruit growers 
Objective : To compensate for the losses suffered by producers of potatoes and citrus fruits in the Algarve, Ribatejo and Oeste regions as a result of the drought which has devastated Portugal since November 2004 
Legal basis : Decreto-lei n.o 96/2005 de 9 de Junho de 2005 e projecto de portaria de aplicação do auxílio relativos à criação de uma linha de crédito destinada aos produtores do sector horto-frutícola que tenham sofrido perdas de produção 
Budget : EUR 1,35 million 
Aid intensity or amount : Per-hectare aid may compensate losses in less-favoured areas at a rate of up to 12,52 % for citrus fruits and 6,58 % for potatoes, and in other areas at a rate of up to 5,47 % for citrus fruits and 5,27 % for potatoes 
Duration : Ad hoc aid measure. The measure in question is scheduled to apply until the end of 2006 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/
