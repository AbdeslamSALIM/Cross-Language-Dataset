Member State: Spain 
Aid No: N 583/04 
Title: National Programme for the extension of broadband in rural and remote areas 
Objective: To support the provision of broadband services, at conditions similar to urban areas, in certain rural and remote areas of Spain which are currently not served and where there are no plans for coverage in the near future. 
Legal basis: Proyecto de Orden por la que se regulan las bases, el régimen de ayudas y la gestión y se realiza la convocatoria del programa de extensión de la banda ancha en zonas rurales y aisladas 
Overall budget: 
Total amount of direct grants: EUR 26,4 million; 
Total amount of loans: EUR 111,9 million 
Aid intensity: unknown, depending on offers providers and finalisation of contract with selected provider, maximum 30 % of the eligible cost 
Duration of project: 2005-2008 
Other information: The selected providers will be mandated to provide non-discriminatory wholesale access to third party operators.
