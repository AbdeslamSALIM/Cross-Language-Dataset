Removal from the register of Case C-101/03 [1] 
(2005/C 296/35) 
(Language of the case: Italian) 
By order of 24 June 2005, the President of the Court of Justice of the European Communities has ordered the removal from the register of Case C-101/03 (reference for a preliminary ruling Tribunale di Milano, Sezione Prima Penale): in criminal proceedings against Alfonso Galeazzo, Marco Banatti. 
[1] OJ C 101, 26.4.2003.
