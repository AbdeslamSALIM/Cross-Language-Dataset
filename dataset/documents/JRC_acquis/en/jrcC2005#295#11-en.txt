Prior notification of a concentration 
(Case COMP/M.4041 — Basell/Société du Craqueur de l'Aubette) 
(2005/C 295/11) 
(Text with EEA relevance) 
1. On 18 November 2005, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Basell Holdings B.V. (%quot%Basell%quot%, the Netherlands), belonging to the group Access Industries (%quot%Access%quot%, United States) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of Société du Craqueur de l'Aubette S.N.C (%quot%SCA%quot%, France) and a butadiene production plant (%quot%Butadiene plant%quot%, France) and related assets from the group Shell (%quot%Shell%quot%, the Netherlands), by way of purchase of shares and assets. 
2. The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: 
- for Basell: Manufacture and sale of polyolefins and related products; 
- for Access: Group of companies principally active in the oil, coal, aluminium, power and telecommunications sector; 
- for SCA: Manufacture and sale of ethylene and propylene; 
- for the Butadiene plant: Manufacture and sale of butadiene. 
3. On preliminary examination, the Commission finds that the notified transaction could fall within the scope of Regulation (EC) No 139/2004. However, the final decision on this point is reserved. 
4. The Commission invites interested third parties to submit their possible observations on the proposed operation to the Commission. 
Observations must reach the Commission not later than 10 days following the date of this publication.
