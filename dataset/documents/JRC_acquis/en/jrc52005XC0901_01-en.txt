Member State: United Kingdom (Wales) 
Aid No: N 627/2003 
Title: Pilot Agri-Environment Entry-Level Scheme (Wales) 
Objective: To test new agri-environmental proposals on a variety of farming circumstances before opening the scheme to farmers across all Wales 
Legal basis: Section 1 of the 1986 Agriculture Act and Section 40 and 85 of the 1998 Government of Wales Act 
Budget: GBP 80000 per year for years 2004 to 2009 
Aid intensity or amount: 
Payments of aid for agri-environmental commitments under agreements will not exceed the maximum amounts as required by Article 24(2) to Council Regulation (EC) No 1257/1999 [1] and its Annex. 
Up to 100 % of eligible costs of aid for provision of technical support. Aid per beneficiary will not go beyond EUR 100000 over any three year period or 50 % of eligible cost whichever is greater 
Duration: 5 years from the date of the approval by the Commission 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/

Aid intensity or amount: Variable according to submeasure 
Duration: Until 31.12.2006
