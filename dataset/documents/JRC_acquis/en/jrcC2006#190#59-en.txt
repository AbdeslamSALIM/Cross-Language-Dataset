Case F-27/05: Judgment of the Civil Service Tribunal (First Chamber) of 28 June 2006 — Le Maire v Commission (Officials — Remuneration — Refusal to grant the daily subsistence allowances) 
Judgment of the Civil Service Tribunal (First Chamber) of 28 June 2006 — Le Maire v Commission 
(Case F-27/05) [1] 
Parties 
Applicant: Viviane Le Maire (Brussels, Belgium) (represented by: G. Bournéou and F. Frabetti, lawyers) 
Defendant: Commission of the European Communities (represented by: J. Currall, Agent) 
Re: 
Annulment of the decision of the Commission of the European Communities refusing the applicant the right to the daily subsistence allowances provided for in Article 10 of Annex VII to the Staff Regulations of officials of the European Communities, following her entry into service on 16 February 2004 
Operative part of the judgment 
The Tribunal: 
1. Dismisses the action as inadmissible. 
2. Orders the Commission of the European Communities to bear its own costs and half of the costs of the applicant. 
[1] OJ C 182 of 23.07.2005 (case initially registered before the Court of First Instance of the European Communities under number T-191/05 and transferred to the European Civil Service Tribunal by order of 15.12.2005).
