Prior notification of a concentration 
(Case COMP/M.4311 — Philips/BenQ/PBDS) 
Candidate case for simplified procedure 
(2006/C 161/02) 
(Text with EEA relevance) 
1. On 3 July 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which Koninklijke Philips Electronics N.V. (%quot%Philips%quot%, Netherlands) and BenQ Corporation (%quot%BenQ%quot%, Taiwan), acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of Philips %amp% BenQ Digital Storage Corporation (%quot%PBDS%quot%, Taiwan) by way of transfer of assets. 
2. The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: 
- for Philips: design, manufacture and sale of broad range of electronic products, including drives and media for CD and DVD, 
- for BenQ: communications and multimedia devices, 
- for PBDS: development, manufacture and sale of optical data storage drives (ODD) for personal computers. 
3. On preliminary examination, the Commission finds that the notified concentration could fall within the scope of Regulation (EC) No 139/2004. However, the final decision on this point is reserved. Pursuant to the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [2] it should be noted that this case is a candidate for treatment under the procedure set out in the Notice. 
4. The Commission invites interested third parties to submit their possible observations on the proposed operation to the Commission. 
Observations must reach the Commission not later than 10 days following the date of this publication. Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M. 4311 — Philips/BenQ/PBDS to the following address:
