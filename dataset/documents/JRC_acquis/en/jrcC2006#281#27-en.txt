Case C-331/06: Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Rechtbank Amsterdam (Netherlands) lodged on 31 July 2006 — K.D. Chuck v Raad van bestuur van de Sociale Verzekeringsbank 
Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Rechtbank Amsterdam (Netherlands) lodged on 31 July 2006 — K.D. Chuck 
v Raad van bestuur van de Sociale Verzekeringsbank 
Referring court 
Rechtbank Amsterdam 
Parties to the main proceedings 
Applicant: K.D. Chuck 
Defendant: Raad van bestuur van de Sociale Verzekeringsbank 
Question referred 
Where a worker is resident outside the Community on the date on which he reaches pensionable age, should Article 48 of the Regulation [1] be applied in the same way as in a case where the worker concerned is resident in the territory of the Community? 
[1] Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 of the Council of 14 June 1971 on the application of social security schemes to employed persons and their families moving within the Community (English special edition: Series I Chapter 1971(II) p. 416).
