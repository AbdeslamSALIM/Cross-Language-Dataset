Taking of the oath by a new Member of the Court of Justice 
(2006/C 143/01) 
Following his appointment as Advocate General at the Court of Justice of the European Communities by decision of the Representatives of the Governments of the Member States of the European Communities of 6 April 2006 [1], Mr Paolo Mengozzi took the oath before the Court on 3 May 2006. 
[1] OJ L 104 of 13 April 2006, p. 36
