the beatles here revisit all the great classics of rock'n'roll, from chuck berry to little richard, with all the ardor of youth and an incredible talent. whether the songs live or studio, which seem moreover recorded in conditions close to live, they are as jewels, traces of the first golden age of the beatles. you hear young people full of talent united by the same passion for rock'n'roll. a sister volume to his impeccable gratifying, essential for all beatles fans such as chuck berry or the stones first!
