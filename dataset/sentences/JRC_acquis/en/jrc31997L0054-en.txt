Article 2 Member States shall adopt and publish the measures necessary to comply with this Directive before 23 September 1998.
They shall apply the measures from 23 September 1998.
Article 3 This Directive shall enter into force on the 20th day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
Article 4 This Directive is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 23 September 1997.
