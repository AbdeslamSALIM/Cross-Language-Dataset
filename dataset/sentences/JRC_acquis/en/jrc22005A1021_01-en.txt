Article 7 Goods in transit 1.
The provisions of the Euro-Mediterranean Agreement may be applied to goods exported from either Tunisia to one of the new Member States or from one of the new Member States to Tunisia, which comply with the provisions of Protocol No 4 and which on the date of accession are either en route or in temporary storage, in a customs warehouse or in a free zone in Tunisia or in that new Member State. 2.
