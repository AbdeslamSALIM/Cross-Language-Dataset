Article 4 Research and development aid 1.
Public aid for research and development to the shipbuilding, conversion and repair industry may be considered compatible with the common market where this public assistance relates to: (i) fundamental research; (ii) basic industrial research, provided that the aid intensity is limited to 50 % of eligible costs; (iii) applied research, provided that the aid intensity is limited to 35 % of eligible costs; (iv) development, provided that the aid intensity is limited to 25 % of eligible costs. 2.
