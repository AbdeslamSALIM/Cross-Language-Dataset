The Administrative Board shall, by 31 March each year at the latest, send the Commission an estimate of revenue and expenditure.
The budgetary authority shall draw up the establishment plan of the Foundation.'; 4.
The financial provisions applying to the Foundation shall be adopted pursuant to Article 209 of the Treaty.
The Administrative Board shall, not later than 31 March, send the revenue and expenditure accounts, the analysis of financial management and the balance sheet of the Foundation for the preceding financial year to the European Parliament, the Council, the Commission and the Court of Auditors.
The Court of Auditors shall transmit to the authorities responsible for giving discharge and to the Commission, by 30 November at the latest, it annual report accompanied by the replies of the Foundation to its comments, and shall ensure publication thereof in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
Done at Luxembourg, 30 June 1993.
