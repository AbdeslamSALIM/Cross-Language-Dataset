Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4327 — Egis/Alpine/Hochtief-Austrian Highway A5) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4327 — Egis/Alpine/Hochtief-Austrian Highway A5) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 188/02) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 3 August 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which Egis Projects S.A. (%quot%Egis Projects%quot%, France), Alpine Mayderer Bau GmbH (%quot%Alpine%quot%, Austria) and HOCHTIEF PPP Solutions GmbH (%quot%HOCHTIEF PPP%quot%, Germany), acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of Bonaventura Straßenerhaltungs-GmbH (%quot%Bonaventura Straßenerhaltung%quot%, Austria) by way of purchase of shares in a newly created company constituting a joint venture. 2.
