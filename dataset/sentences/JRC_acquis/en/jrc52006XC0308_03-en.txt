Notice inviting applications for authorisation to prospect for hydrocarbons in block Q11 of the Dutch continental shelf (Text with EEA relevance) Notice inviting applications for authorisation to prospect for hydrocarbons in block Q11 of the Dutch continental shelf (2006/C 56/07) (Text with EEA relevance) The Minister for Economic Affairs of the Kingdom of the Netherlands hereby gives notice that an application has been received for authorisation to prospect for hydrocarbons in block Q11 as indicated on the map appended as Annex 3 to the Mining Regulation (Mijnbouwregeling) (Government Gazette (Staatscourant) 2002, No 245).
