State Aid — Invitation to submit comments pursuant to Article 88(2) of the EC Treaty concerning aid — C 52/2005 (ex NN 88/2005) — Italy — Subsidy to digital decoders (Text with EEA relevance) State Aid Invitation to submit comments pursuant to Article 88(2) of the EC Treaty concerning aid C 52/2005 (ex NN 88/2005) — Italy — Subsidy to digital decoders (2006/C 118/04) (Text with EEA relevance) By means of the letter dated 21 December 2005, reproduced in the authentic language on the pages following this summary, the Commission notified Italy of its decision to initiate the procedure laid down in Article 88(2) of the EC Treaty concerning the abovementioned aid.
Interested parties may submit their comments within one month of the date of publication of this summary and the following letter, to: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition State aid Greffe B-1049 Brussels Fax No: (32-2) 296 12 42 These comments will be communicated to Italy.
