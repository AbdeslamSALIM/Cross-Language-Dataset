Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4404 — Universal/BMG Music Publishing) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4404 — Universal/BMG Music Publishing) (2006/C 276/02) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 3 November 2006 the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Universal Music Group Inc. (%quot%Universal%quot%, USA), belonging to the group Vivendi S.A. (%quot%Vivendi%quot%, France), acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking BMG Music Publishing Group (%quot%BMG Music Publishing%quot%, Germany et al.) which currently forms part of the Bertelsmann group, by way of purchase of shares and assets. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Vivendi: media, telecommunication, and music industry; - for Universal: music publishing and music recording; - for BMG Music Publishing: music publishing. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4404 — Universal/BMG Music Publishing, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. --------------------------------------------------
