Case T-6/06: Action brought on 13 January 2006 — Mopro-Nord GmbH v Commission Action brought on 13 January 2006 — Mopro-Nord GmbH v Commission Parties Applicant: Mopro-Nord GmbH (Altentreptow, Germany) (represented by: L.
Pleas in law and main arguments The applicant contests Commission Decision C(2005) 3310 final of 6 September 2005 relating to State aid No N 363/2005 for the construction of a whey refining plant.
It also complains of a breach of the obligation to give reasons under Article 253 EC and infringement of the principles of legal security and certainty.
Finally, the applicant submits that the Commission's decision infringes the principle of protection of legitimate expectations and the prohibition of discrimination.
