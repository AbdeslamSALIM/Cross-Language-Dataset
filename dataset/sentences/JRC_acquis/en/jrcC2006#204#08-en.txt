Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4091 — Linde/Spectra) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4091 — Linde/Spectra) (2006/C 204/08) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 16 August 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 and following a referral pursuant to Article 4(5) of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Linde AG (%quot%Linde%quot%, Germany) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking Spectra Gases, Inc. (%quot%Spectra%quot%, USA) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
