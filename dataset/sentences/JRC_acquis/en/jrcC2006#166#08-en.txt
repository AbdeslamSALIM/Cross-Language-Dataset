Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4258 — IVECO/AFIN) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4258 — IVECO/AFIN) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 166/08) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 6 July 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 and following a referral pursuant to Article 4(5) of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Iveco International Trade Finance SA (%quot%IITF%quot%, Italy) belonging to the group IVECO, which is itself controlled by the group FIAT, acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of AFIN Leasing A.G. (%quot%AFIN%quot%, Austria) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4258 — IVECO/AFIN, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
