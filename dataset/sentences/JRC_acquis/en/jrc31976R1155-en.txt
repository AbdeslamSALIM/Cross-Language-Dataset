PACKAGING AND PRESENTATION A.
Presentation The foliage of Asparagus sprengeri shall be made up into bunches of 100 g, 250 g or multiples of 250 g.
Fern must not be broken and must be without side growth.
PACKAGING AND PRESENTATION A.
Commercial specifications - class, - the indication %quot%fern%quot% or %quot%clipped fern%quot%, - number of bunches and number of stems per bunch, - minimum and maximum length or code of size, - the indication %quot%blond%quot% or %quot%biondo%quot% should the occasion arise.
