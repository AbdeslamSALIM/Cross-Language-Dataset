Article 4 Member States shall present to the Commission appropriate qualitative information about concentration levels of heavy metals present in packaging within the meaning of Article 11 of Directive 94/62/EC and on the presence of noxious and other hazardous substances and materials within the meaning of the third indent of point 1 of Annex II to Directive 94/62/EC.
Article 5 Member States shall present to the Commission the completed formats set out in this Decision, together with an appropriate description of how data have been compiled as well as the main characteristics of the databases from which the data are extracted.
Article 6 The data to be included in Annex III (Tables 3, 4.1 and 4.2) concerning the weight of packaging waste recycled or recovered refer to the inputs of packaging waste to an effective recycling or recovery process.
The provision of the data required in the column headed 'sorted for recycling` shall be voluntary.
Article 8 The Commission, in accordance with the procedure laid down in Article 21 of Directive 94/62/EC, will review the framework for Member State provision of data, in order to make these data comparable and consistent.
Article 9 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
