TITLE II YEAR 2003 Article 4 Financial contribution from the Community The Community may grant Member States, for the year 2003, a financial contribution (hereinafter %quot%financial contribution%quot%) for their expenditure incurred under Article 3.
The financial contribution shall be calculated in accordance with the rates set out in Table 3, Group 1 of Annex IV to Regulation (EC) No 2792/1999.
Article 6 Procedure 1.
Member States shall submit their applications for the payment of the expenditure by 30 June 2004.
The Commission shall pay up to 50 % of the assistance on receipt of the application and the balance after certification by the authorities referred to in paragraph 2. 2.
Done at Brussels, 20 December 2002.
