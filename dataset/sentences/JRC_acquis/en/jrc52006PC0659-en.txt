Implementation periods range from 0 to 12 years with an average of 4.4 years.
As regards trading rights, Viet Nam will grant all enterprises with foreign direct investment full trading rights no later than 1 January 2007, except for the products subject to State trading.
For specified products however, full trading rights will be granted two years later, on 1 January 2009.
Viet Nam will also replace the current import ban of large motorcycles with engine capacity exceeding 175 cm3 with a non-automatic licensing system by 31 May 2007.
