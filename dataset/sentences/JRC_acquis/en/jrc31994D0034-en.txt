Article 4 If a local unit in a Member State is unable to use the Animo computerized network as from 1 June 1994, the central authority of that Member State shall ensure that all messages which that unit is intended to handle under the Animo system are taken over by the central unit.
Article 5 The provisions of this Decision shall apply without prejudice to earlier provisions concerning the Animo system, in particular those contained in Decisions 92/486/EEC and 93/227/EEC.
Article 6 The situation concerning the implementation of the Animo system will be re-examined in March 1994.
Article 7 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 24 January 1994.
