Article 2 Animal Disease Notification System An amount of EUR 115000 is hereby authorised for maintenance of the notification system referred to in Decision 2005/176/EC and the acquisition of dedicated mapping software for it.
Article 3 Information measures relating to animal welfare 1.
An amount of EUR 450000 is hereby authorised for the following measures in the animal welfare area: - a study to draw up specifications for a navigation system as referred to in Chapter VI, point 4, of Annex I to Regulation (EC) No 1/2005, - a study on developing information and communication technologies linked to satellite navigation systems to step up the effectiveness of official checks in this area. 2.
An amount of EUR 250000 is hereby authorised for carrying out a Eurobarometer-style survey of consumer attitudes to livestock welfare. 3.
An amount of EUR 145000 is hereby authorised for the publication by the Commission of information on Community animal protection legislation.
Article 4 The measures provided for in Articles 1, 2 and 3 shall be financed and the contractors selected under the existing framework contract.
Done at Brussels, 5 August 2005.
