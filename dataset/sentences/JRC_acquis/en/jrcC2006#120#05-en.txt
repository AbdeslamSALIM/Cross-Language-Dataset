Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4205 — Aleris International/Corus Group) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4205 — Aleris International/Corus Group) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 120/05) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 11 May 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 and following a referral pursuant to Article 4(5) of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Aleris International, Inc. (%quot%Aleris%quot%, USA) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of parts of the aluminium business (%quot%The Target Business%quot%) of the undertaking Corus Group plc (%quot%Corus%quot%, UK), by way of purchase of shares. 2.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4205 — Aleris International/Corus Group, to the following address: European Commission Competition DG Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
