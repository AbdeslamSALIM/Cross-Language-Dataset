Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M. 4106 — Isola/Polyclad) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M. 4106 — Isola/Polyclad) (2006/C 64/02) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 9 March 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 and following a referral pursuant to Article 4(5) of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1], by which Isola Group SARL (%quot%Isola%quot%, USA), jointly controlled by TPG Partners IV and T3 II, acquires, within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation, sole control over the Polyclad business of Cookson Group plc (UK), Cookson Overseas Limited (UK), Cookson Electronics Limited (Hong Kong), Cookson Investments, Inc.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4106 — Isola/Polyclad to the following address: European Commission Competition DG Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. --------------------------------------------------
