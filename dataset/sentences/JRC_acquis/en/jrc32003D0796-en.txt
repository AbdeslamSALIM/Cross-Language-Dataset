Article 2 Membership of the Group 1.
Until 1 July 2004, if a Member State has not designated one or more competent bodies with the function of regulatory authorities, that Member State shall be represented in the Group by a representative of another competent public authority. 4.
Article 3 Organisation of the Group 1.
The Group shall elect a chairperson from among its members. 2.
Article 4 Consultation The Group shall consult extensively and at an early stage with market participants, consumers and end-users in an open and transparent manner.
Article 5 Confidentiality Without prejudice to the provisions of Article 287 of the Treaty, where the Commission informs the Group that the advice requested or the question raised is of a confidential nature, members of the Group as well as observers and any other person shall be under an obligation not to disclose information which has come to their knowledge through the work of the Group or its working groups.
Article 6 Repeal Decisions 95/539/EC and 92/167/EEC are repealed.
