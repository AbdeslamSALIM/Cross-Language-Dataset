Non-opposition to a notified concentration (Case COMP/M.4007 — Reckitt Benckiser/Boots Healthcare International) Text with EEA relevance Non-opposition to a notified concentration (Case COMP/M.4007 — Reckitt Benckiser/Boots Healthcare International) (2006/C 92/06) (Text with EEA relevance) On 6 January 2006, the Commission decided not to oppose the above notified concentration and to declare it compatible with the common market.
