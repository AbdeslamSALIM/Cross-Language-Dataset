ANNEX MODEL OF THE STANDARD CONSIGNMENT NOTE drawn up pursuant to Article 42 of Council Regulation (EEC) No 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community INTRODUCTION This standard consignment note was drawn up to apply the provisions of Council Regulation (EEC) No 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community.
It will be made available to the competent authorities designated by the Member States pursuant to Article 38 of the abovementioned Regulation so that they can implement the appropriate procedure for the supervision and control of waste shipments.
