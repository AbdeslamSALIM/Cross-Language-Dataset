Article 2 The fishing opportunities set out in the Protocol shall be allocated among the Member States as follows: (a) tuna seiners: Spain : 18 vessels France : 21 vessels Italy : 1 vessel (b) surface longliners: Spain : 20 vessels Portugal : 5 vessels If licence applications from these Member States do not cover all the fishing opportunities set out in the Protocol, the Commission may take into consideration licence applications from any other Member State.
Article 4 This Regulation shall enter into force on the third day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Done at Brussels, 18 January 2005.
