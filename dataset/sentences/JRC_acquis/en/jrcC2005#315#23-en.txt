Case T-354/05: Action brought on 19 September 2005 — TF1 v Commission of the European Communities Action brought on 19 September 2005 — TF1 v Commission of the European Communities Parties Applicant(s): Télévision Française 1 SA (Boulogne, France) (represented by: J.-P.
Smits, lawyers) Defendant(s): Commission of the European Communities Form of order sought The applicant(s) claim(s) that the Court should: - annul the decision given by the Commission on 20 April 2005 regarding the licence fee system in favour of France Télévision; - make an order as to costs in accordance with the law.
The applicant challenges the Commission's interpretation of the decision in Altmark [1] and its application in the present case.
