Applications for import licences shall be for a quantity not less than 100 tonnes and not more than 1 000 tonnes of rice. 2.
Notwithstanding Article 10 of Commission Regulation (EC) No 1162/95 (7), the amount of the security in respect of the import licences shall be equal to 25 % of the value of the customs duties calculated in accordance with Article 11 of Regulation (EC) No 3072/95 applicable on the date of the application. 3.
Article 7 Regulation (EEC) No 2942/73 is hereby repealed.
Article 8 This Regulation shall enter into force on the seventh day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
