Representativeness of the results For structural variables, the relative standard error (without taking design effect into account) of any yearly estimate representing 1 % or more of the working age population shall not exceed: (a) 9 % for countries with a population between 1 million and 20 million inhabitants and (b) 5 % for countries with a population of 20 million or more.
Countries with less than 1 million inhabitants are exempted from these precision requirements and structural variables should be collected for the total sample unless the sample meets requirements expressed in (a).
