The release into free circulation of bananas falling within CN code 08030019 at the common customs tariff rate of duty shall be subject to the presentation of an import licence issued by the Member States to any party applying therefor, irrespective of their place of establishment in the Community. 2.
The security shall be EUR 15 per tonne.
Article 3 This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
It shall apply from 1 January 2006.
