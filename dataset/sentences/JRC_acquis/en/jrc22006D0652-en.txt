Article 2 This Decision shall enter into force on the day of its adoption by the Joint Committee.
However: - provisions of this Decision which insert the reference to Regulation (EC) No 631/2004, as well as those regarding Point 3(b) of Annex II to the Agreement, extending the possibility of exemption from Swiss compulsory insurance for pensioners residing in Portugal shall take effect on 1 June 2004; - provisions of this Decision regarding Point 4 of Annex II to the Agreement offering access to healthcare in Switzerland to persons who are covered by the Swiss sickness insurance scheme and who reside in France shall take effect on 1 January 2004.
Done at Brussels, 6 July 2006.
