If the total sum of a security required expressed in the currency of the Member State concerned has increased owing to a modification of the agricultural conversion rate for the security entering into force on the date of the operative event, the security is accepted on the basis of the agricultural conversion rate in force the day before the modification if the deficit amounts to less than ECU 20 expressed in national currency using the new agricultural conversion rate. 3.
An offer for which a tender security lodged showing a deficit of ECU 20 or more owing to a modification as referred to in paragraph 2, shall be accepted as valid for the quantity actually covered by that security, unless before the moment at which the offers are examined with a view to deciding on the result of the tender, the tenderer undertakes in writing to supplement the deficit and does so within five working days following the closing date of the tender, except in cases of force majeure.
