Article 3 Modalities for the definition of statistical reporting requirements In defining and imposing its statistical reporting requirements, the ECB shall specify the actual reporting population within the limits of the reference reporting population as defined in Article 2.
Article 4 Obligations of Member States Member States shall organise themselves in the field of statistics and shall fully cooperate with the ESCB in order to ensure the fulfilment of the obligations arising out of Article 5 of the Statute.
Article 5 Regulatory power of the ECB 1.
Article 6 Right of verification and compulsory collection of statistical information 1.
Article 7 Imposition of sanctions 1.
Article 8 Confidentiality regime 1.
Transmission from the national central banks to the ECB of confidential statistical information shall take place to the extent and at the level of detail necessary for the exercise of the tasks to be carried out through the ESCB, as described in Article 105 of the Treaty. 3.
Article 9 Final provisions This Regulation shall enter into force on the day of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
The remaining Articles shall apply from 1 January 1999.
