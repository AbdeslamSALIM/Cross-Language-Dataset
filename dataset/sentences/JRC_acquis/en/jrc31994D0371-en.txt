Article 5 Only eggs packaged in small or large packs, in accordance with the requirements of Regulation (EEC) No 1907/90 and (EEC) No 1274/91, or egg products in accordance with Directive 89/437/EEC may be used in catering kitchens including restaurants and for the preparation of non industrial scale egg products or products containing eggs.
Article 7 This Decision shall be reviewed before 30 June 1996, following an opinion by the Scientific Veterinary Committee on the time/temperature couples to be respected for the deposit and transport in accordance with Article 18 of Directive 92/118/EEC.
Article 8 This Decision shall apply at the latest on 1 January 1995.
Article 9 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Luxembourg, 20 June 1994.
