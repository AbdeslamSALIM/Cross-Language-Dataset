Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3975 — Cargill/DFI) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3975 — Cargill/DFI) (2005/C 270/03) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 21 October 2005, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Cargill Inc. (%quot%Cargill%quot%, USA) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertakings Degussa Food Ingredients GmbH and Maxens GmbH (altogether %quot%DFI%quot%, Germany) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Cargill: Animal feed, food and food ingredients, pharmaceuticals, - for DFI: Food ingredients. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.3975 — Cargill/DFI, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 BE-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. --------------------------------------------------
