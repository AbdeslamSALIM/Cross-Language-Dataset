The Committee of the Regions' views on the draft directive: THE COMMITTEE OF THE REGIONS: 1.1 endorses the draft Directive, subject to the changes proposed below; 1.2 stresses the importance of adopting, in addition to the measures applying to admission of third-country nationals to carry out scientific research, further coordinating measures to achieve the objective of investing 3 % of Member States' GDP in research before the end of the decade.
Brussels, 17 November 2004.
