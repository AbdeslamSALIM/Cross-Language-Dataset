Commission Regulation (EC) No 1779/2005 should therefore be repealed, HAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION: Article 1 Reopening of fishery The fishery for sprat in ICES zone IIIa by vessels flying the flag of Denmark or registered in Denmark should be reopened on 28 November 2005.
Article 2 Repeal Commission Regulation (EC) No 1779/2005 is hereby repealed.
Article 3 Entry into force This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
It shall apply from 28 November 2005.
Done at Brussels, 20 December 2005.
