This catalogue lists all the varieties the seeds of which, pursuant to Article 16 of the abovementioned Directive, are not subject — in the EEA — to marketing restrictions relating to variety, except in the cases provided for in Articles 16(2) and 18 of the abovementioned Directive.
The common catalogue of varieties of vegetable species was published for the first time on 29 June 1972 [5].
This 24th complete edition updates the 23rd complete edition [6].
The catalogue comprises four columns: — column 1: %quot%Variety%quot% — column 2: %quot%EU country of admission%quot% — column 3: %quot%EFTA country of admission%quot% (for the time being only Norway) — column 4: %quot%Observations%quot% Column 1 lists all the varietal names in alphabetical order.
Column 1 Immediately below the conventional name, the other names of the variety by which it has been officially admitted in Member States or EFTA country are repeated in alphabetical order preceded by a dash, only one per country of admission being given.
Columns 2 and 3 - The letters %quot%a%quot% or %quot%b%quot% appear before the abbreviation of each Member State or EFTA country having officially admitted a variety.
