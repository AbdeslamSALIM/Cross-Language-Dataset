The delivered products must: - in the case of unprocessed dried grapes, meet the minimum requirements laid down in the Annex to Regulation (EC) No 1621/1999, - in the case of unprocessed dried figs, meet the minimum requirements laid down in the Annex to Commission Regulation (EC) No 1573/1999(15) and have a minimum size of: - 180 fruits per kg up to the end of the 2003/04 marketing year, and - 150 fruits per kg in subsequent marketing years.
The specific industrial uses referred to in paragraph 1 shall include in particular the production of preparations of a kind used in animal feeding covered by CN code 2309, roasted coffee substitutes covered by CN code 2101 30 and alcohol covered by CN code 2208.
