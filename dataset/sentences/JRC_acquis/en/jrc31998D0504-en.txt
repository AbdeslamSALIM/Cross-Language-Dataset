Article 2 The President of the Council shall give the notification provided for in Article 19 of the Agreement on behalf of the European Community.
The President of the Council shall, in accordance with Article 8 of the Agreement, preside over the Joint Council and present the position of the Community.
In accordance with Article 10 of the Agreement, a representative of the Commission assisted by representatives of the Member States shall preside over the Joint Committee and present the position of the Community.
Article 4 This Decision shall be published in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
Done at Luxembourg, 29 June 1998.
