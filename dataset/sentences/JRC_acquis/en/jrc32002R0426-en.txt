The conversion rates to be applied to: - the amounts of a structural or environmental nature referred to in Article 5 of Regulation (EC) No 2799/98, - the maximum amount per hectare of the marketing aid in the nut and locust bean sector fixed in Article 2 of Regulation (EEC) No 790/89, - the premiums and payments in the sheepmeat sector referred to in Articles 4, 5 and 11 of Regulation (EC) No 2529/2001, shall be as set out in Annex I.
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on the seventh day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
Done at Brussels, 7 March 2002.
