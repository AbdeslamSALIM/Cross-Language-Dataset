(5) In view of this risk assessment, the budgetary stance in the programme is sufficient to maintain the Stability and Growth Pact's medium-term objective of a position of close-to-balance throughout the programme period (with a temporary deviation in 2004 related to the one-off accounting adjustments).
It also provides a sufficient safety margin against breaching the 3 % of GDP deficit threshold with normal macroeconomic fluctuations over the period covered in the update. (6) The debt ratio is estimated to have declined to 49,1 % of GDP in 2004, well below the 60 % of GDP Treaty reference value.
