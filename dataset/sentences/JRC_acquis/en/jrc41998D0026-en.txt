The assessment shall cover the following areas in particular: - checks at the external borders, in particular the application of the Common Manual, - surveillance of external land and sea borders, - visas, in particular the application of the Common Consular Instructions, - conditions governing the movement of aliens, including measures taken with regard to illegal immigration and aliens illegally on Schengen territory, - residence permits and alerts for the purpose of refusing entry, - police cooperation, - mutual assistance in criminal matters, including extradition, - drugs, - SIS, in particular the application of the Sirene Manual, - protection of personal data, - policy on expulsion and readmission, - arrangements for movement within airports.
Confidentiality The Committee members and the experts shall regard as confidential the information they acquire in the course of performing their duties.
