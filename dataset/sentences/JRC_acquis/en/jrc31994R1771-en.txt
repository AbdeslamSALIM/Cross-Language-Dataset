The prohibition on the introduction into the Community of the pelts of the animal species listed in Annex I to Council Regulation (EEC) No 3254/91 and of the other goods listed in Annex II to that Regulation, shall enter into force on 1 January 1996. 2.
The Commission shall, in accordance with the procedure laid down in Article 5 of Council Regulation (EEC) No 3254/91, determine before 1 September 1995: (a) which countries meet the condition of Article 3 (1) of that Regulation and (b) the appropriate forms for certification referred to in Article 4 thereof.
