No later than 30 September 2006, the Commission shall present to the Council a report on the allocation of quota within the Community, accompanied by appropriate proposals.
No later than 31 December 2006, the Council, acting on the basis of Article 37 of the Treaty, shall decide on the proposals from the Commission on the basis of the report referred to in paragraph 1. 3.
It shall apply from 1 July 2005.
Done at Brussels, 30 May 2005.
