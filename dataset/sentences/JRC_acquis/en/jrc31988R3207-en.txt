Article 2 Regulation (EEC) No 4000/87 is hereby repealed.
Article 3 The Commission, in accordance with the procedure provided for in Article 17 of Regulation (EEC) No 2771/75, shall make the necessary technical adjustments to Council or Commission Regulations concerning the common organization of the markets in eggs which result from the application of Article 1.
Article 4 This Regulation shall enter into force on the day of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
It shall apply with effect from 1 January 1988.
Done at Luxembourg, 17 October 1988.
