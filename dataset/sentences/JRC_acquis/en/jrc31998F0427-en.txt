Article 2 Review of performance Without prejudice to the mechanism for evaluating the application and implementation at national level of international undertakings in the fight against organised crime, established in Joint Action 97/827/JHA (4), each Member State shall periodically review compliance with its Statements made in accordance with Article 1.
Article 4 Review The Council shall review this Joint Action in the light of the results of the operation of the mechanism for evaluating the application and implementation at national level of international undertakings in the fight against organised crime, established in Joint Action 97/827/JHA.
Article 5 Entry into force This Joint Action shall enter into force on the day of its publication.
Article 6 Publication This Joint Action shall be published in the Official Journal.
Done at Luxembourg, 29 June 1998.
