Article 2 Initiation of an infringement procedure 1.
Article 3 Powers of the ECB and the competent national central bank 1.
Article 4 Assistance by the authorities of the Member States 1.
Article 5 Notice of objections 1.
The ECB or the competent national central bank, as the case may be, shall notify the undertaking concerned in writing of the factual results of any inquiry carried out and of the objections raised against the undertaking concerned before any decision is taken on the imposition of a sanction. 2.
Article 6 Rights and obligations of the undertaking concerned 1.
The undertaking concerned shall have the right to legal representation during the infringement procedure. 4.
Article 7 Confidentiality of the infringement procedure 1.
Article 8 Review of the decision by the Governing Council of the ECB 1.
The Governing Council of the ECB shall fix a time-limit by which the information shall be provided, but this shall be no fewer than 10 working days.
Article 9 Enforcement of the decision 1.
The decision of the ECB shall establish the manner in which payment of the sanction is to be made. 3.
Article 10 Simplified procedure for minor infringements 1.
This Article shall be without prejudice to the procedure applicable in the event of non-compliance with minimum reserve requirements as laid down in Article 11 of this Regulation.
Article 11 Procedure in the event of non-compliance with minimum reserve requirements 1.
The time limit foreseen in Article 8(2), shall be reduced to five working days. 2.
Article 12 Time limits 1.
Without prejudice to Article 4 of the Council Regulation, the time limits foreseen in this Regulation shall run from the day following receipt of a communication or delivery thereof by hand.
Done at Frankfurt am Main, 23 September 1999.
On behalf of the Governing Council of the ECB The President Willem F.
