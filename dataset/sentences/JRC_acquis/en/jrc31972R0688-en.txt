The minimum export prices provided for in Article 7 of Regulation (EEC) No 234/68 shall be fixed each year not later than: - 31 March for begonias, sinningias, gladioli, dahlias and the lilium species; - 31 January for other products.
In paragraph 3 of the same Article, 1 March and 1 January shall be substituted for 1 December and 15 October respectively.
