The Council decided, on 24 September 2004, to appoint the members and alternate members of the European Social Fund Committee, on a proposal from the Commission for the period from 1 May 2004 to 30 April 2007.
The term of office of these replacements also expires on 30 April 2007. 5.
Given the change in the composition of the Committee, and the revised mandate of the Committee for the period 2007-13, and having consulted the Member States, the Commission submits to the Council the present proposal for a Council Decision appointing members and alternate members of the European Social Fund Committee for the period from 1 January 2007 to 31 December 2009.
Article 2 Decision 2005/C 252/01 is repealed from 31 December 2006.
Article 3 This Decision shall be published, for information, in the Official Journal of the European Union .
Regulation as last amended by Regulation (EC) No. 173/2005 (OJ L 29, 2.2.2005, p.3).
