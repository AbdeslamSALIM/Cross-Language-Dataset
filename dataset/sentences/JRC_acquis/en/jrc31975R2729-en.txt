The import levy applicable to mixtures composed either of rice classifiable under several different processing groups or stages or of rice classifiable under one or more different processing groups or stages and of broken rice shall be that applicable: - to the component predominating by weight, when that component represents at least 90 % of the weight of the mixture, - the component liable to the higher or highest import levy, when no component represents at least 90 % of the weight of the mixture.
Done at Luxembourg, 29 October 1975.
