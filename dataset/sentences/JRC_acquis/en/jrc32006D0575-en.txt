Article 2 Animal diseases notification system An amount of EUR 200000 is hereby authorised for maintenance of the notification system referred to in Decision 2005/176/EC.
Article 3 Communication relating to animal welfare 1.
An amount of EUR 200000 is hereby authorised for the study on developing information and communication technologies linked to satellite navigation systems to step up the effectiveness of official checks in the animal welfare area. 2.
An amount of EUR 240000 is hereby authorised for the communication by the Commission to consumers of information on Community animalprotection legislation. 3.
An amount of EUR 230000 is hereby authorised for the communication by the Commission to trading partners of developments in Community animalprotection legislation.
Article 4 Contract award procedure Contractors will be selected on the basis of invitations to tender to be issued in autumn 2006.
