This Decision establishes a common technical regulation covering the attachment requirements for TETRA equipment accessing emergency services in the frequency bands in the range 380 MHz to 383 MHz and 390 MHz to 393 MHz.
The reference to the standard is set out in the Annex. 2.
National type-approval regulations covering equipment within the scope of the harmonised standard referred to in the Annex shall be repealed with effect from 15 December 1999. 2.
Article 5 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 15 September 1999.
