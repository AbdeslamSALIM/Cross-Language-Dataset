Article 4 Member States shall determine what penalties are applicable when national provisions enacted pursuant to this Directive are infringed and shall take all necessary measures to ensure that they are implemented.
Article 6 This Directive shall enter into force on the date of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Article 7 This Directive is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 18 July 2005.
