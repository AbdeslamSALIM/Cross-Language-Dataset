Article 5 Member States shall not impede for reasons relating to electromagnetic compatibility the placing on the market and the taking into service on their territory of apparatus covered by this Directive which satisfies the requirements thereof.
Where a Member State ascertains that apparatus accompanied by one of the means of attestation provided for in Article 10 does not comply with the protection requirements referred to in Article 4, it shall take all appropriate measures to withdraw the apparatus from the market, prohibit its placing on the market or restrict its free movement.
The conformity of apparatus with that described in the technical file shall be certified in accordance with the procedure laid down in paragraph 1.
Article 13 This Directive is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 3 May 1989.
