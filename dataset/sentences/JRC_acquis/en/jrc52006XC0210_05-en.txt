State aid — Greece — State aid No C 47/2005 (ex NN 86/2005) — ELVO (Hellenic Vehicle Industry S.A.) — Invitation to submit comments pursuant to Article 88(2) of the EC Treaty (Text with EEA relevance) State aid — Greece State aid No C 47/2005 (ex NN 86/2005) — ELVO (Hellenic Vehicle Industry S.A.) Invitation to submit comments pursuant to Article 88(2) of the EC Treaty (2006/C 34/06) (Text with EEA relevance) By means of the letter dated 7.12.2005 reproduced in the authentic language on the pages following this summary, the Commission notified Greece of its decision to initiate the procedure laid down in Article 88(2) of the EC Treaty concerning the abovementioned measure.
Interested parties may submit their comments to the measure in respect of which the Commission is initiating the procedure within one month of the date of publication of this summary and of the following letter, to: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition State aid Greffe SPA 3, 6/5 B-1049 Brussels Fax No: (32-2) 296 12 42 These comments will be communicated to Greece.
TEXT OF SUMMARY PROCEDURE On 27 May 2002 the Commission received a complaint alleging aid granted to ELVO — Hellenic Vehicle Industry S.A. (hereinafter %quot%ELVO%quot%), Greece.
On 16 December 1999 Greece adopted Act No 2771/99.
According to Greece, the aid was used as working capital to cover production requirements for defence purposes exclusively and it should be covered by the derogation under Article 296 of the EC Treaty.
Derogation under Article 296 of the EC Treaty According to the Greek authorities, the measure should fall under Article 296 of the EC Treaty, since ELVO produces mainly for the Greek Army.
