ANNEX TECHNICAL ACTION PLAN 2003 FOR IMPROVING AGRICULTURAL STATISTICS (TAPAS 2003) The aims of the activities to be carried out in 2003 under the technical action plan for improving agricultural statistics (TAPAS) are to contribute to improve agricultural statistics in the following fields: (i) data on the physical aspects of European agriculture; (ii) the environmental aspects of agricultural accounting; (iii) the agri-environmental indicators, including the use of pesticides; (iv) rural development.
