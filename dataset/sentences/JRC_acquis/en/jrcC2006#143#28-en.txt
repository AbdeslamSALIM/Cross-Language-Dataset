Case C-495/04: Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 30 March 2006 (reference for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden) — A.C.
Smits-Koolhoven v Staatssecretaris van Financiën (Directive 95/59 — Tax on the consumption of manufactured tobacco — Herbal cigarettes — Used exclusively for medical purposes) Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 30 March 2006 (reference for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden) — A.C.
Smits-Koolhoven v Staatssecretaris van Financiën (Case C-495/04) [1] Referring court Hoge Raad der Nederlanden Parties to the main proceedings Applicant: A.C.
Smits-Koolhoven Defendant: Staatssecretaris van Financiën Re: Reference for a preliminary ruling — Hoge Raad der Nederlanden — Interpretation of Art. 7(2) of Council Directive 95/59/EC of 27 November 1995 on taxes other than turnover taxes which affect the consumption of manufactured tobacco (OJ 1995 L 291, p. 40 — Medicinal cigarettes — Exclusively medical purpose — Therapeutic or prophylactic purposes Operative part of the judgment Article 7(2) of Council Directive 95/59/EC of 27 November 1995 on taxes other than turnover taxes which affect the consumption of manufactured tobacco must be interpreted as meaning that cigarettes without tobacco which do not contain substances having a medical effect but which are presented and marketed as an aid to giving up smoking are not %quot%used exclusively for medical purposes%quot% within the meaning of the second subparagraph of that provision.
