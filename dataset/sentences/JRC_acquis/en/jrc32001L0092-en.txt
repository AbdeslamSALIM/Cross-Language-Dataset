CONFORMITY OF PRODUCTION 5.1.
Measures to ensure conformity of production must be taken in accordance with the provisions laid down in Article 10 of Directive 70/156/EEC.
Definition The items in question are those laid down in Article 2 of Regulation No 43 of the United Nations Economic Commission for Europe, in the most recent version adopted by the European Community.
