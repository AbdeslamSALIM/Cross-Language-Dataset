Article 2 Fishery products imported into the Community from Serbia and Montenegro shall meet the requirements set out in Articles 3, 4 and 5.
Article 4 The fishery products shall come from approved establishments, factory vessels, or cold stores, or from registered freezer vessels listed in Annex II.
Article 5 All packages shall bear the words %quot%SERBIA AND MONTENEGRO%quot% and the approval/registration number of the establishment, factory vessel, cold store or freezer vessel of origin in indelible letters, except in the case of frozen fishery products in bulk and intended for the manufacture of preserved foods.
Article 6 This Decision shall apply from 28 February 2004.
Done at Brussels, 23 December 2003.
