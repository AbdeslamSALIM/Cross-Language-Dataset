COMMENTS ON THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE 2005 BUDGET The 2005 budget’s provisional implementation for the most significant articles is influenced by the following factors (NB: in parenthesis, the level of over (+) or under (–) execution is presented). 2.1.
Milk and milk products (– EUR 1 049.3 million) This under-implementation result from the lower expenditure incurred for the different measures due to the favourable internal and external dairy market conditions.
Beef and veal (+ EUR 288.1 million) This over-execution result from the increased number of 2004 animal premiums paid by the Member States as opposed to the number retained in the budget. 2.2.9.
Sub-heading 1b – Rural Development (– EUR 62.6 million) The 2005 commitment appropriations for rural development (EU-15) and for the transitional instrument for the new Member States were set at the ceiling for this subheading.
