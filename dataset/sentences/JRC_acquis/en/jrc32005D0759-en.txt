Member States shall designate the authorities referred to in paragraph 1 which is responsible for such checks and immediately inform the Commission thereof. 3.
Article 3 This Decision shall not apply to the movement onto Community territory of birds accompanying their owners from Andorra, Faeroe Islands, Greenland, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, San Marino or Switzerland.
Article 4 Member States shall immediately take the necessary measures to comply with this Decision and publish those measures.
Article 5 This Decision shall apply until 30 November 2005.
Article 6 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
