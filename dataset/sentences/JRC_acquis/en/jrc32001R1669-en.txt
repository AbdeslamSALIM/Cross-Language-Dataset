The statistical threshold referred to in Article 12 of the Basic Regulation shall be fixed, for each type of goods, so that imports or exports exceeding EUR 1000 in value or 1000 kg in net mass shall be collected for the production of external trade statistics. 2.
Application by the Member States of the threshold referred to in paragraph 1 shall remain optional. 3.
It shall apply from 1 January 2002.
Done at Brussels, 20 August 2001.
