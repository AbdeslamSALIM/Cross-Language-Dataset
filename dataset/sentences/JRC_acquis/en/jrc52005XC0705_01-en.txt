The Commission asked for additional information by letters dated 27 February, 6 June, 9 September and 10 December 2003, to which Germany replied by letters dated 26 March, 2 April, 28 July, 30 October and 22 December 2003 respectively.
The decision was communicated to Germany by letter dated 19 February.
Germany took note of the decision by letter dated 24 March and added that the planned aid would be significantly higher than the notified amount.
Germany took note of the decision by letter dated 8 June.
The Commission asked for additional information by letter dated 15 October.
The Commission notes that, under Article 8 of Council Regulation (EC) No 659/1999, [2] the Member State concerned may withdraw a notification in due time before the Commission has taken a decision on the aid.
Consequently, the Commission has decided to close the formal investigation procedure initiated under Article 88(2) of the EC Treaty in respect of the abovementioned aid, since Germany has withdrawn its notification." [1] OJ C 87, 7.4.2004, p. 5, and C 211, 21.8.2004, p. 8.
