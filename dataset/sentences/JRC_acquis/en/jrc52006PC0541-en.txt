Proposal for a Council Directive amending Council Directive 91/414/EEC to include carbendazim as active substance /* COM/2006/0541 final */ [pic] | COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES | Brussels, 14.9.2006 COM(2006) 541 final Proposal for a COUNCIL DIRECTIVE amending Council Directive 91/414/EEC to include carbendazim as active substance (presented by the Commission) EXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM Conclusions: The attached draft proposal for a Council Directive concerns the inclusion under strict conditions of carbendazim as active substance in the positive list (Annex I) of Council Directive 91/414/EEC concerning the placing of plant protection products on the market.
The draft Directive is not subject to the right of scrutiny of the European Parliament (Article 8 of Council Decision 1999/468/EC).
