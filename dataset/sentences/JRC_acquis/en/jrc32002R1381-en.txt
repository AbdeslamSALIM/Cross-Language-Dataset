The tariff quota for the marketing year 2002/03 is to be equal to 85313 tonnes, white sugar equivalent, for products of CN code 1701 11 10.
The following global tariff quotas at zero duty of products of CN code 1701 11 10, expressed as white sugar equivalent, are opened for the following marketing years for imports originating in a country that according to Annex I to Regulation (EC) No 2501/2001 benefits from the special arrangements for least developed countries: - 85313 tonnes for the marketing year 2002/03, - 98110 tonnes for the marketing year 2003/04, - 112827 tonnes for the marketing year 2004/05, and - 129751 tonnes for the marketing year 2005/06.
All Common Customs Tariff duties, as well as any additional duties referred to in Article 24 of Regulation (EC) No 1260/2001, on imports under these quotas are suspended.
