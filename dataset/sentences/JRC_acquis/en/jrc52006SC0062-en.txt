Protocol 31 to the EEA Agreement contains specific provisions on the cooperation between the Community and the EEA EFTA States outside the four freedoms. 2.
The attached draft Decision of the EEA Joint Committee (concerning Decision NÂ° 1554/2005/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 7 September 2005), aims to modify Protocol 31 to extend cooperation in the field of Social policy.
