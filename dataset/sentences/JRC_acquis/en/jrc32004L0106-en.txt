Article 3 References to Directive 77/799/EEC in respect of excise duties shall be construed as being made to Regulation (EC) No 2073/2004.
Member States shall bring into force the laws, regulations and administrative provisions necessary to comply with this Directive before 30 June 2005.
They shall apply these provisions from 1 July 2005.
By way of derogation from Article 2 of Directive 2004/56/EC, Member States are not obliged to adopt and apply the provisions needed to comply with Directive 2004/56/EC in respect of excise duties.
Article 5 This Directive shall enter into force on the twentieth day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Article 6 This Directive is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 16 November 2004.
