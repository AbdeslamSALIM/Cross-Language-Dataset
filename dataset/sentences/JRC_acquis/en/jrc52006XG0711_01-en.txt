Information concerning the extension of the Grains Trade Convention, 1995 Information concerning the extension of the Grains Trade Convention, 1995 (2006/C 160/01) At its 21st session (London, 14 June 2005), the International Grains Council decided, under the provisions of the Grains Trade Convention, 1995 [1], to extend the Convention for two years until 30 June 2007. [1] OJ L 21, 27.1.1996, p. 47. --------------------------------------------------
