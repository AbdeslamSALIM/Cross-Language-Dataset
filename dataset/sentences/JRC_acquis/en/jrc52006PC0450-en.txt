OBJECTIVE OF THE COMMISSION PROPOSAL The Commission proposal is to establish a European support programme, MEDIA 2007, following on from the current MEDIA Plus and MEDIA Training programmes with a proposed budget of € 1.055 billion.
Response to Parliament’s amendments adopted at first reading The Commission fully accepted the majority of the Parliament's amendments: 1, 2, 4, 8, 10, 11, 12, 16, 17, 19, 20, 25, 29, 32, 36, 37, 44, 46, 50, 58, 59, 60, 62, 66, 72 and 75.
The Commission accepted in principle or partially the following amendments: 5, 6, 7, 9, 14, 18, 21, 22, 23, 24, 30, 35, 38, 40, 41, 42, 43, 45, 47, 48, 53, 54, 56, 61, 63, 67, 68, 76 and 77.
The Commission rejected the following amendments: 3, 15, 26, 27, 28, 31, 33, 34, 39, 49, 51, 52, 55, 57, 64, 65, 69, 70, 71, 73, 74, 78, 80, 81, 82 and 83.
