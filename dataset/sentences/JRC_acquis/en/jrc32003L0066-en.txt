The provisions in Annexes I, II and III requiring the giving of information relating to noise shall apply only where that information is required by Member States under Article 3 of Directive 86/594/EEC.
Article 3 Member States shall adopt and publish the provisions to comply with this Directive no later than 30 June 2004.
Article 4 This Directive shall enter into force on the 20th day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Article 5 This Directive is addressed to the Member States.
