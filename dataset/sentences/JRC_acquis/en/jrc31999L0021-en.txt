Article 2 Member States shall ensure that dietary foods for special medical purposes may be marketed within the Community only if they comply with the rules laid down in this Directive.
The name under which dietary foods for special medical purposes are sold shall be respectively: - in Spanish: %quot%Alimento dietético para usos médicos especiales%quot% - in Danish: %quot%Levnedsmiddel/Levnedsmidler til særlige medicinske formål%quot% - in German: %quot%Diätetisches/Diätetische Lebensmittel für besondere medizinische Zwecke (Bilanzierte Diäten)%quot% - in Greek: %quot%Διαιτητικά τρόφιμα για ειδικούς ιατρικούς σκοπούς%quot% - in English: %quot%Food(s) for special medical purposes%quot% - in French: %quot%Aliment(s) diététique(s) destiné(s) a des fins médicales spéciales%quot% - in Italian: %quot%Alimento dietetico destinato a fini medici speciali%quot% - in Dutch: %quot%Dieetvoeding voor medisch gebruik%quot% - in Portuguese: %quot%Produto dietético de use clínico%quot% - in Finnish: %quot%Kliininen ravintovalmiste/kliinisiä ravintovalmisteita%quot% - in Swedish: %quot%Livsmedel för speciella medicinska ändamål%quot%. 2.
