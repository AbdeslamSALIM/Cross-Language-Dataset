Chelyuskin, Semion Ivanovich () (c. 1700 - 1764) was a Russian polar explorer and naval officer.Chelyuskin graduated from the Navigation School in Moscow.
He first became a deputy navigator while serving in the Baltic Fleet (1726) and later promoted to navigator (1733).
He participated in expeditions, led by Vasily Pronchischev and Khariton Laptev.
