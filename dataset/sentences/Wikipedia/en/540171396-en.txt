They were led by a Basque Juan Sebastián Elcano, who took over command of the expedition after Magellan's death.
The fleet reached Río de la Plata on January 10, 1520.On March 30, the crew established a settlement they called Puerto San Julian.
As a result, Victoria with some of the crew sailed west for Spain.
