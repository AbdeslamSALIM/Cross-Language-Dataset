| current champions = Olympique Lyonnais | most successful club = Olympique de Marseille(10 times) | website = FFF - Coupe de France This is about the football competition, for the rugby, see Coupe de France (rugby).Coupe de France, or Coupe Charles Simon, is a competition of French football clubs, open to all professional and non-professional teams.
Three players have won the cup five times: Marceau Sommerlynck (1946, 1947, 1948, 1953 and 1955), Dominique Bathenay (1974, 1975, 1977, 1982 and 1983) and Alain Roche (1986, 1987, 1993, 1995 and 1998).Since 1927, the President of France has always attended the championship match and presented the cup to the winning team's captain.
