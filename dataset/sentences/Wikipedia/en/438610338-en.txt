Imre, Croatian: Mirko/Emerik, Slovak: Imrich I.), (1174 &ndash; 30 September/30 November 1204), King of Hungary and Croatia (1182-1204), Duke of Croatia and Dalmatia (1194-1196).
His father had Emeric crowned on 16 May 1182 in order to ensure his succession.
The king could count on the support of Pope Innocent III who continuously tried to persuade the duke to fulfill his father's last will and organize a Crusader.
