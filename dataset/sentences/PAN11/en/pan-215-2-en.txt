Andronico sent the two Ambassadors to Roger arranging the marriage, and brought him the insignia of Grand Duke, which is the same between us General of the sea: great dignity of the empire, but not the majors.
Points paid the Emperor to the people of war, and makes many honors and favors to his captains.
Andronico said the pay according to the difference of the arms and occupation, four ounces of silver each month to the men at arms, their horses Thin two, and so the pilots and people of mandoneros an ounce, and provided that they reach the coast of a province of the Empire they were given four pay, and when they wanted to return to their homes together, or split, you fought two for the trip
