Semantic Links and Integrity Constraints The semantic relations between lexical units help structuring the lexicon on the paradigmatic plane.
These well known relations are often described in two types: the hierarchical relations (hyponymy, hyperonymy, meronymy and holonymy) and the equivalence/opposition relations (synonymy, antonymy).
