After Algirdas Brazauskas became the secretary of the party, Kirkilas became his press secretary.Since the independence was declared on March 11 1990, Kirkilas was involved in the state matters and was elected four times (in 1992, 1996, 2000, and 2004) to the Seimas under the Social Democratic Party of Lithuania ballot.
