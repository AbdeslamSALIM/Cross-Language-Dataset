The town was devastated, though the Biscayan assembly and the Oak of Guernica survived.
Pablo Picasso painted his famous &quot;Guernica&quot; painting to commemorate the horrors of the bombing and René Iché made a violent sculpture the day after the bombing.In 1943, Ajangiz (opposite river Oka) was incorporated without any name change.
