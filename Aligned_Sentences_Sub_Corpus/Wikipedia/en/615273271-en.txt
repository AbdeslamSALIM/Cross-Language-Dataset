The dorsal fin is short, blunt and triangular.
It is lightly coloured all over - slightly more white on the underside than the back.Length is about 1 m at birth and 2.3 m at full maturity.
Lifespan is about 30 years.The Irrawaddy Dolphin is a slow swimmer.
