It is also the capital of the Rabat-Salé-Zemmour-Zaer region.The city is located on the Atlantic Ocean at the mouth of the river Bou Regreg.
In 1146, the Almohad ruler Abd al-Mu'min turned Rabat's ribat into a full scale fortress to use as a launching point for attacks on Spain.
