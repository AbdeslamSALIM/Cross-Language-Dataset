It was capital of the County of Tyrol from 1418 to 1848.After the county had been handed over to the Habsburg family in 1363 after the abdication of Countess Margarete Maultasch of Tyrol, in 1420 Friedrich IV moved the court to Innsbruck.
The important mint was also moved to Hall in 1577.The Tyrolean struggle for freedom of 1809 drew attention again to Merano.
