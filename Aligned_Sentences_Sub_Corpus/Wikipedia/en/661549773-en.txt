It is formed at the confluence of the rivers Crişul Alb (Hungarian: Fehér-Körös) and Crişul Negru (Hungarian: Fekete-Körös) near Gyula (Romanian: Ghiula).
The Crişul Repede River (Hungarian: Sebes-Körös) flows into the river Körös near Gyoma.
