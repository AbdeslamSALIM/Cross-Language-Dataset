Retrieved on 14 August 2007.Nantes is the préfecture of the Pays de la Loire région, as well as the Loire-Atlantique département.
When the French Revolution broke out, Nantes chose to be part of it, although the whole surrounding region soon degenerated into an open civil war against the new republic known as the War in the Vendée.
In the subsequent 20 years, many service sector organisations moved into the area, but economic difficulties forced most of these to close.
