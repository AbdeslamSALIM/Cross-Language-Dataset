Adam Opel GmbH (commonly known as Opel) is a German automaker, part of General Motors.
Its Zafira people carrier was sold in Japan badged as a Subaru Traviq, while the Omega was sold in the US as the Cadillac Catera for model years 1997 to 2001.
The Pontiac LeMans (1989-1994), which was the first car produced by Daewoo in South Korea for export to North America, was based on the Opel Kadett E (now Astra).Opels appeared under their own name in the USA from 1958 to 1975, when they were sold through Buick dealers as captive imports.
