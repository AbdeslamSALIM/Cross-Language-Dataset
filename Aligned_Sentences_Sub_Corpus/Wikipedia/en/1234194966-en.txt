She resumed singing at age 17.Mirella made her operatic debut in Modena at 1955, at age 19, as Micaëla in Bizet's Carmen.
She was offered many roles after this, but she decided to put her career aside and marry her singing teacher, Leone Magiera, and have a child.She resumed her career in 1958 by winning a singing competition and singing Mimì in Puccini's La Bohème at the Teatro Regio in Torino.
