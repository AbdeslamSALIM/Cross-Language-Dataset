Zionism is an international political movement that originally supported the reestablishment of a homeland for the Jewish People in Palestine (Hebrew: Eretz Yisra'el, “the Land of Israel”), and continues primarily as support for the modern state of Israel."An international movement originally for the establishment of a Jewish national or religious community in Palestine and later for the support of modern Israel."
