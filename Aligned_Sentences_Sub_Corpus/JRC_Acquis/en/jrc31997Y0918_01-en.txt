Decision of 21 March 1997 on public access to European Environment Agency documents Decision of 21 March 1997 on public access to European Environment Agency documents (97/C 282/04) Explanatory memorandum 1.
An application for access to an Agency document shall be sent in writing to the Executive Director of the Agency (5).
This Decision shall take effect on 1 June 1997.
It shall be published in the Official Journal of the European Communities and made available to the public. (1) OJ L 120, 11. 5. 1990, p. 1. (2) OJ L 158, 23. 6. 1990, p. 56.
