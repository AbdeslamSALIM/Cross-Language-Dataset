Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3998 — Axalto/Gemplus) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3998 — Axalto/Gemplus) (2006/C 79/03) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 24 March 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 and following a referral pursuant to Article 4(5) of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Axalto Holding NV (%quot%Axalto%quot%, The Netherlands) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking Gemplus International SA (%quot%Gemplus%quot%, Luxemburg) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
