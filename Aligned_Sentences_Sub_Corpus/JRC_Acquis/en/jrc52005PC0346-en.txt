On 24 April 2002, Monsanto asked to limit the request to food and food ingredients produced from genetically modified organisms.
In the light of Article 46(1) of this Regulation, requests submitted under Regulation (EC) No 258/97 before 18 April 2004 and for which an additional assessment report has been transmitted to the Commission before that date, shall be processed under Regulation (EC) No 258/97.
