Imposition by France of a public service obligation for scheduled air services between Strasbourg and Frankfurt (Text with EEA relevance) Imposition by France of a public service obligation for scheduled air services between Strasbourg and Frankfurt (2006/C 246/10) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
The public service obligation is as follows: 2.1.
Continuity of service Except in cases of force majeure, the number of flights cancelled for reasons directly attributable to the carrier must not exceed 2 % of the number of flights scheduled in any IATA scheduling season.
