Member States shall adopt and publish, by 22 June 2005 at the latest, the laws, regulations and administrative provisions necessary to comply with this Directive.
They shall apply these provisions from 23 June 2005.
Article 3 This Directive shall enter into force on the 20th day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Article 4 This Directive is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 15 December 2004.
