In the EU's 25 Member States it is at local and regional level that the proposed measures to limit the environmental impact of vehicle traffic must be put into practice. 3.
As the European Commission correctly states in its proposal for a directive, the public sector's share of the market for vans and lorries is only 6 %, but around 30 % for buses.
The Committee has reservations about the mandatory requirement contained in the proposed Directive that public bodies should procure 25 % of heavy duty vehicles on the basis of clean vehicle standards.
