In accordance with Commission notice No 2004/C 248/06 published in the Official Journal on 7 October 2004, the Portuguese Government has revised the fares and freight tariffs for scheduled air services on which public service obligations have been imposed operated on the following routes: - Lisbon/Ponta Delgada/Lisbon, - Lisbon/Terceira/Lisbon, - Lisbon/Horta/Lisbon, - Funchal/Ponta Delgada/Funchal, - Porto/Ponta Delgada/Porto, - Lisbon/Santa Maria/Lisbon, - Lisbon/Pico/Lisbon. 2.
