Article 2 Establishment of areas A and B 1.
The affected Member State shall ensure that no live poultry and live birds other than poultry, and hatching eggs thereof are: (a) dispatched from areas A and B to other Member States and to third countries; (b) dispatched from areas A and B to the remaining part of the national territory of the affected Member State; (c) transported within areas A and B; and (d) moved between areas A and B. 2.
The affected Member State shall ensure that no products other than hatching eggs of the species referred to in paragraph 1 and of wild feathered game are: (a) dispatched from areas A and B to other Member States and to third countries; (b) dispatched from areas A and B to the remaining part of the national territory of the affected Member State; and (c) transported between areas A and B.
Article 4 Derogations for live birds and day-old chicks 1.
The animal health certificates in accordance with Model 2 of Annex IV to Council Directive 90/539/EEC accompanying consignments of day-old chicks referred to in paragraph 2(c) and (e) shall include the words: %quot%The consignment complies with the animal health conditions laid down in Commission Decision 2006/135/EC.%quot% 6.
Article 5 Derogations for hatching eggs 1.
The affected Member State shall ensure that the products referred to in paragraphs 1(b), (c) and 2(b), (c), (d) are accompanied by commercial documents stating: %quot%The consignment complies with the animal health conditions laid down in Commission Decision 2006/135/EC.%quot% Article 7 Derogations for eggs for human consumption and egg products 1.
The affected Member State shall ensure that consignments of eggs for human consumption referred to in paragraph 1(a) are accompanied by commercial documents stating: %quot%The consignment complies with the animal health conditions laid down in Commission Decision 2006/135/EC.%quot% Article 8 Derogation for animal by-products 1.
Article 9 Conditions for movements 1.
Where movements of animals or products thereof covered by this Decision are authorised under Articles 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8, the authorisation shall be based on the favourable outcome of a risk assessment carried out by the competent authority and all appropriate biosecurity measures shall be taken to avoid the spread of avian influenza. 2.
Article 10 Compliance and Information All Member States shall immediately adopt and publish the measures necessary to comply with this Decision.
Article 11 Addressee This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
