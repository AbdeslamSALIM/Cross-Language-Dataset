The Member States shall bring into force the laws, the regulations and administrative provisions necessary to conform with this Directive no later than 1 July 2003.
Article 3 This Directive shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Article 4 This Directive is addressed to the Member States.
