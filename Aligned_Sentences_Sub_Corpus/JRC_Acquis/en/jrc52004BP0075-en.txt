European Parliament resolution on the financial perspective with a view to the European Council meeting in December 2004 P6_TA(2004)0075 Financial Perspective European Parliament resolution on the financial perspective with a view to the European Council meeting in December 2004 The European Parliament, - having regard to the communications from the European Commission (COM(2004)0101 and COM(2004)0487), - having regard to the EC Treaty, and in particular Article 272 thereof, - having regard to the Interinstitutional Agreement of 6 May 1999, and in particular Article 26 thereof [1], - having regard to the Treaty establishing a Constitution for Europe, - having regard to its question for oral answer with debate O-0067/2004 (B6-0130/2004) to the Council, - having regard to Rule 108(5) of its Rules of Procedure, A.
whereas the current financial perspective will remain in force until the end of the year 2006, B.
whereas Article 272 of the EC Treaty provides for the adoption of annual budgets even in the absence of a financial perspective, D.
Confirms its objective of having a "multiannual financial framework for the period after 2006"; 2.
Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council and the Commission. [1] OJ C 172, 18.6.1999, p. 1.
Agreement as amended by Decision 2003/429/EC (OJ L 147, 14.6.2003, p. 25). --------------------------------------------------
