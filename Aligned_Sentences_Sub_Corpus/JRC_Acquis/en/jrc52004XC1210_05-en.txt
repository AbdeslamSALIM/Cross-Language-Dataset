The United Kingdom has decided to modify the public service obligations in respect of the scheduled air services between the Orkney Mainland (Kirkwall) and the islands of Westray, Sanday, Stronsay and Eday as set out in the Notice published in the Official Journal of the European Communities C 363 of 19 December 2001, as modified in the Official Journal of the European Union C 295 of 5 December 2003, pursuant to Article 4(1)(a) of Council Regulation (EEC) No 2408/92 of 23 July 1992 on access for Community air carriers to intra-Community air routes. 2.
The price of a single adult fare must not exceed GBP 31 on each route.
No other changes may be made to the fare levels without the prior written consent of Orkney Islands Council.
