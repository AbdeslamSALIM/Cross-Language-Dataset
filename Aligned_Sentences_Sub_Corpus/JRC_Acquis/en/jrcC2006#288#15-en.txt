Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4492 — Candover/Ferretti) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4492 — Candover/Ferretti) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 288/15) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 16 November 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Candover Partners Limited UK controlled by Candover Investments plc (%quot%Candover%quot%, UK) acquire(s) within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking Ferretti S.pA. (%quot%Ferretti%quot%, Italy) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Candover: private equity investor; - for Ferretti: design, production and sales of luxury motor boats. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4492 — Candover/Ferretti, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004 p. 1 [2] OJ C 56, 05.3.2005 p. 32 --------------------------------------------------
