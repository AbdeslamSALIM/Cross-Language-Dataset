Article 2 In the case of dumping or public aids liable to warrant the Community applying the measures provided for in Article 33 of the Cooperation Agreement and Article 25 of the Interim Agreement, the introduction of anti-dumping or countervailing duties shall be decided upon in accordance with the procedure and detailed rules laid down in Regulation (EEC) No 459/68.
However, the second indent of the second paragraph of Article 3 shall not apply to products covered by such Regulations.
Article 5 The Commission shall notify the Cooperation Council and the Joint Committee as laid down in Article 35 of the Cooperation Agreement and Article 27 of the Interim Agreement.
Article 6 This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
