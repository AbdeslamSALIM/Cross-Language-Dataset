During the years 2000, 2001 and 2002, it shall be prohibited to land or retain on board sand eels caught within the geographical area bounded by the east coast of England and Scotland, and a line sequentially joining the following coordinates: - the east coast of England at latitude 55° 30'N, - latitude 55° 30'N, longitude 1° 00'W, - latitude 58° 00'N, longitude 1° 00'W, - latitude 58° 00'N, longitude 2° 00'W, - the east coast of Scotland at longitude 2° 00'W. 2.
Before 1 March 2001 and again before 1 March 2002, the Commission will report to the Council on the effects of the provision contained in paragraph 1.
Done at Luxembourg, 8 June 2000.
