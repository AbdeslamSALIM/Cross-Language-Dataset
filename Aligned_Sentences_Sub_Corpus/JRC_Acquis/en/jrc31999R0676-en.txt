The Member States shall send the Commission, by 15 May 1999, a list of horizontal belt driers granted approval before the beginning of the 1999/2000 marketing year and consequently eligible for the derogation provided for in Article 1(1).
Article 3 This Regulation shall enter into force on the seventh day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
It shall apply from the date of its entry into force, with the exception of Article 1(1), which shall apply from 1 April 2000.
Done at Brussels, 26 March 1999.
