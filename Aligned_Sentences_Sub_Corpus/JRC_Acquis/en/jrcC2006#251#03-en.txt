Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4412 — Permira Holdings Ltd/BorsodChem — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4412 — Permira Holdings Ltd/BorsodChem Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 251/03) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 9 October 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which Permira Holdings Ltd (Guernsey) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of BorsodChem Nyrt (%quot%BorsodChem%quot%, Hungary) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Permira Holdings Ltd: Holding company for undertakings active in a variety of sectors, including ingredients for plastic production; - for BorsodChem: production of certain plastics and plastic ingredients. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4412 — Permira Holdings Ltd/BorsodChem, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
