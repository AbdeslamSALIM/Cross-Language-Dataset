Article 4 Member States shall produce the series referred to in Articles 1 and 2 of this Regulation, for the data concerning the 2001 reference year and subsequent years for the characteristics 21 12 0 and 21 14 0 of Annex 2 and the characteristics listed in section 4 of Annex 6 to Regulation (EC, Euratom) No 58/97.
Member States shall produce the data-series referred to in Article 3 of this Regulation in respect of the data on the 2002 reference year and subsequent years for the characteristics listed in section 4 of Annex 7 to Regulation (EC, Euratom) No 58/97.
