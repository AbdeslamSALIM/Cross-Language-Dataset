Proposal for a Council Regulation laying down specific measures for agriculture in favour of the smaller Aegean islands and amending Regulation (EC) No 1782/2003 /* COM/2006/0264 final - CNS 2006/0093 */ [pic] | COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES | Brussels, 2.6.2006 COM(2006) 264 final 2006/0093 (CNS) Proposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION laying down specific measures for agriculture in favour of the smaller Aegean islands and amending Regulation (EC) No 1782/2003 (presented by the Commission) EXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM On 19 July 1993, the Council adopted Regulation (EEC) No 2019/93 introducing specific measures for the smaller Aegean islands concerning certain agricultural products.
To ensure coordination of the respective arrangements concerning the smaller Aegean islands Regulation (EC) No 1782/2003 should therefore be amended. CHAPTER II SPECIFIC SUPPLY ARRANGEMENTS Article 3 Forecast supply balance 1.
CHAPTER III MEASURES TO ASSIST LOCAL AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS Article 7 Support measures 1.
Article 10 Monitoring The procedures and physical and financial indicators for ensuring effective monitoring of the implementation of the support programme shall be adopted in accordance with the procedure referred to in Article 15(2).
CHAPTER IV ACCOMPANYING MEASURES Article 11 State Aid 1.
For the agricultural products, to which Articles 87, 88 and 89 of the Treaty thereof apply, the Commission may authorise operating aid in the sectors producing, processing and marketing those products, with a view to mitigating the specific constraints on farming in the smaller islands as a result of their remoteness, insularity and distant location. 2.
CHAPTER V FINANCIAL PROVISIONS Article 12 Financial resources 1.
The Community shall finance the measures provided for in Chapters II and III up to an annual maximum of EUR 23.93 million. 3.
CHAPTER VI GENERAL, TRANSITIONAL AND FINAL PROVISIONS Article 13 Draft support programme 1.
Greece shall submit a draft support programme to the Commission in the framework of the financial allocation referred to in Article 12(2) and (3) by 31 October 2006 at the latest.
The Commission shall evaluate the support programme proposed and decide on its approval in accordance with the procedure referred to in Article 15(2). 3.
Article 14 Implementing rules The measures necessary for the implementation of this Regulation shall be adopted in accordance with the procedure referred to in Article 15(2).
Article 15 Management Committee 1.
The Commission shall be assisted by the Management Committee for Direct Payments established by Article 144 of Regulation (EC) No 1782/2003. 2.
Where reference is made to this paragraph, Articles 4 and 7 of Decision 1999/468/EC shall apply.
Article 16 National measures Greece shall take the measures necessary to ensure compliance with this Regulation, in particular as regards checks and administrative penalties, and shall inform the Commission thereof.
Greece shall communicate to the Commission not later than 15 February each year the appropriations made available to them which they intend to spend in the following year on implementation of the support programme. 2.
Greece shall submit to the Commission, not later than 30 June each year, a report on the implementation of the measures provided for in this Regulation over the previous year. 3.
Not later than 31 December 2011, and thereafter every five years, the Commission shall submit a general report to the European Parliament and the Council showing the impact of the action taken under this Regulation, accompanied if applicable by appropriate proposals.
Article 18 Repeal Regulation (EEC) No 2019/93 is hereby repealed as from 1 January 2007.
Article 19 Transitional measures In accordance with the procedure provided for in Article 15(2), the Commission may adopt the necessary transitional measures to ensure a smooth transition from the measures under Regulation (EEC) No 2019/93 to the measures introduced by this Regulation.
However, Articles 11, 13 and 14 shall apply from the date of its entry into force.
