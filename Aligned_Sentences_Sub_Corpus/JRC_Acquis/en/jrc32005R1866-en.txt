The definitive Regulation thus amended definitive anti-dumping duties imposed by Regulation (EC) No 348/2000 [3] and repealed the possibility of exemption from the duties provided for in Article 2 of Regulation (EC) No 348/2000 (original measures). (2) By Decision 2005/133/EC [4] (the Decision), the Commission suspended the definitive anti-dumping duties partially for a period of nine months, with effect from 18 February 2005.
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Done at Brussels, 8 November 2005.
