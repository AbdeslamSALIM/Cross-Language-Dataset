Notice of initiation of an anti-dumping proceeding concerning imports of trichloroisocyanuric acid (TCCA) originating in the United States of America(2004/C 253/03) The Commission has received a complaint pursuant to Article 5 of Council Regulation (EC) No 384/96(1) (the basic Regulation), alleging that imports of trichloroisocyanuric acid (TCCA), originating in the United States of America (the country concerned), are being dumped and are thereby causing material injury to the Community industry. 1.
Complaint The complaint was lodged on 24 August 2004 by the European Chemical Industry Council (CEFIC) (the complainant) on behalf of producers representing a major proportion, in this case more than 45 % of the total Community production of TCCA. 2.
On this basis, the dumping margin calculated is significant. 4.
