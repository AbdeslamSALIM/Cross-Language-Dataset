Without prejudice to the intervention rules applying, the letters A, B, C, D and E shall be used to identify carcases as from 1 January 1992.
Criteria shall be laid down for differentiating between categories of carcases in accordance with the procedure laid down in Article 27 of Regulation (EEC) No 805/68. 2.
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on the third day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
Done at Luxembourg, 22 April 1991.
