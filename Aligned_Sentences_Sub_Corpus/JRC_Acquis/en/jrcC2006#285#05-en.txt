Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4468 — Candover Partners/Hilding Anders) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4468 — Candover Partners/Hilding Anders) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 285/05) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 10 November 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Candover Partners Limited (%quot%Candover%quot%, UK), through a special purpose vehicle acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking Hilding Anders Holding AB (%quot%Hilding Anders%quot%, Sweden) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
Observations must reach the Commission not later than 10 days following the date of this publication.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4468 — Candover Partners/Hilding Anders, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
