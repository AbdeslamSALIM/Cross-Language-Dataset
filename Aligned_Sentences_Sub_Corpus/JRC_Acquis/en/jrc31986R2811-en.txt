Regulation (EEC) No 1665/72 is hereby amended as follows: 1.1.
Article 1 of Regulation (EEC) No 1546/75 is hereby replaced by the following: 'Article 1 The event within the meaning of Article 5 of Regulation (EEC) No 1676/85 in which the aid in respect of seeds becomes due and payable shall be considered to have occurred on 1 August following the beginning of each marketing year'.
In the table contained in Article 1 of Regulation (EEC) No 1117/79, the following is added: '10.07 C.
Hybrids for sowing.' Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on the third day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
It is applicable from 1 July 1986.
Done at Brussels, 11 September 1986.
