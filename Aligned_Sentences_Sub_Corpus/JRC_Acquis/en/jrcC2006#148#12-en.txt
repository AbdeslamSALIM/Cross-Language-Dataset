Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4278 — PAI Partners SAS/AMEC SPIE SA) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4278 — PAI Partners SAS/AMEC SPIE SA) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 148/12) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 20 June 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which PAI Partners S.A.S. (%quot%PAI%quot%, France), acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of AMEC SPIE S.A. (%quot%AMEC SPIE%quot%, France), currently owned by AMEC plc, via purchase of shares. 2.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (no (32-2) 2964301 or 2967244) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4278 — PAI Partners SAS/AMEC SPIE SA, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition, Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. [2] OJ C 217, 29.7.2000, p. 32; Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/89 has been replaced by Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004. --------------------------------------------------
