Article 4 The certificate shall not be valid until it has been duly stamped by the customs office.
However, a quantity exceeding by no more than 5 % that indicated on the certificate shall be regarded as covered by it.
Article 5 The Member States shall take all necessary measures to check the origin, type, composition and quality of the Emmentaler cheeses for which certificates are issued.
Article 6 This Regulation shall enter into force on the third day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
