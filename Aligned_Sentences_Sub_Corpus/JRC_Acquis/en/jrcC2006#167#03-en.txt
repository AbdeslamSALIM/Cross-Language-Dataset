Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4255 — Evraz/Strategic Minerals) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4255 — Evraz/Strategic Minerals) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 167/03) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 11 July 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertakings Evraz Group S.A. (%quot%Evraz%quot%, Luxembourg) controlled by Crosland Global Ltd (%quot%Crosland%quot%, Cyprus) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control with Sojitz Corporation (%quot%Sojitz%quot%, Japan) of the undertaking Strategic Minerals Corporation (%quot%Strategic Minerals%quot%, USA) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Evraz: vertically-integrated steel production and mining operations mainly in the Russian Federation; - for Sojitz: diversified trading activities and exclusive distribution of vanadium products produced by Strategic Minerals worldwide except for North America; - for Strategic Minerals: mining and processing of vanadium and vanadium products. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4255 — Evraz/Strategic Minerals, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
