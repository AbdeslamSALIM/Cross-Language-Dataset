Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4185 — CVCI/Amber Trust II/AB Sanitas) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4185 — CVCI/Amber Trust II/AB Sanitas) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 123/07) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 16 May 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertakings Citigroup Venture Capital International Jersey Limited (%quot%CVCI%quot%, Jersey) belonging to the group Citigroup Inc (%quot%Citigroup%quot%, USA) and Amber Trust II SCA (%quot%Amber Trust II%quot%, Luxembourg), jointly controlled by Danske Bank A/S(%quot%Danske Bank%quot%, Denmark) and Firebird Private Equity Advisors LLC (%quot%Firebird%quot%, USA) acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of the undertaking AB Sanitas (%quot%Sanitas%quot%, Lithuania) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - CVCI: private equity investment; - Citigroup: global financial services; - Amber Trust II: private equity investment; - Danske Bank: European financial services company; - Firebird: fund management; - Sanitas: pharmaceuticals. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4185 — CVCI/Amber Trust II/AB Sanitas, to the following address: European Commission Competition DG Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
