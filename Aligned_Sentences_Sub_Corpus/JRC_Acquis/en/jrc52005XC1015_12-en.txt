DESCRIPTION (1) The fiscal exemptions are provided for in Article 25 of the Portuguese Estatuto dos Benefícios Fiscais (Tax Relief Regulations), entitled Mais-validas no âmbito do processo de privatização (Capital gains as part of the privatisation process).
ASSESSMENT (2) At this stage, the Commission considers that the measure described in Article 25 of the Portuguese Tax Relief Regulations uses State resources because it is based on the non-perception of corporation taxes which would normally be due to the State.
