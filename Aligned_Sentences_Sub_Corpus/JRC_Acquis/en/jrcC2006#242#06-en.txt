Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4300 — Philips/Intermagnetics) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4300 — Philips/Intermagnetics) (2006/C 242/06) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 29 September 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 and following a referral pursuant to Article 4(5) of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Koninklijke Philips Electronics N.V., part of the group Philips (%quot%Philips%quot%, the Netherlands) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking Intermagnetics General Corporation (%quot%Intermagnetics%quot%, United States) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4300 — Philips/Intermagnetics, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. --------------------------------------------------
