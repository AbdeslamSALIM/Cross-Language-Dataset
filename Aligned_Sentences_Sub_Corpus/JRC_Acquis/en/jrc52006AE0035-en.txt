Traffic and location data may only be processed when they are made anonymous or with the consent of the users. 2.1.4 Article 15 of Directive 2002/58/EC enables Member States to retain data in exceptional cases, where it is deemed necessary, appropriate and proportionate to the prosecution of criminal offences.
Member States were unable to agree on a time limit for such retention in the negotiations on the Directive on data protection in electronic communications and had to abandon attempts to fix a limit. 2.1.5 On 25 March 2004, the European Council considered the possibility of establishing rules on the retention of data generated by providers of electronic communications services.
