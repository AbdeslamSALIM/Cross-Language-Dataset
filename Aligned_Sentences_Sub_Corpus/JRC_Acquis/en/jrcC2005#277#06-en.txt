Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3997 — Sun Capital/Sara Lee) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3997 — Sun Capital/Sara Lee) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2005/C 277/06) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 26 October 2005, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Sun Capital Partners IV, LP, belonging to the Sun Group (%quot%Sun%quot%, USA), acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the European Branded Apparel division (%quot%The Business%quot%) of Sara Lee Corporation (USA), by way of purchase of shares. 2.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.3997 — Sun Capital/Sara Lee, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 BE-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
