Reference to the Annex to the Directive: 7.5.2.2.
Reference to the national legislation: Bekendtgørelse nr. 729 of 15.
When the Danish legislation concerning transport of dangerous goods is amended the Danish authorities will allow such transports on the following conditions: (1) Not more than 25 kg explosive substances under group D are being transported.
