Import licences for rice against applications submitted during the first five working days of October 2006 pursuant to Regulation (EC) No 638/2003 and notified to the Commission shall be issued for the quantities applied for multiplied, where appropriate, by the reduction percentages stipulated in the Annex to this Regulation. 2.
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on 20 October 2006.
Done at Brussels, 19 October 2006.
