Pursuant to Article 1 (4) (i) (1) (b) of Regulation (EEC) No 1576/89, the maximum methyl alcohol content of fruit spirits shall be 1 500 grams per hectolitre of alcohol at 100 % vol obtained from the following fruits: - plum (Prunus domestica L.), - mirabelle (Prunus domestica L.
Done at Brussels, 30 November 1992.
