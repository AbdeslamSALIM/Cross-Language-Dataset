Communication from the Commission to the Council on the rate of contribution to the pension scheme of officials and other servants of the European Communities {SEC(2006)1441} /* COM/2006/0681 final */ [pic] | COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES | Brussels, 14.11.2006 COM(2006) 681 final COMMUNICATION FROM THE COMMISSION TO THE COUNCIL on the rate of contribution to the pension scheme of officials and other servants of the European Communities {SEC(2006)1441} COMMUNICATION FROM THE COMMISSION TO THE COUNCIL on the rate of contribution to the pension scheme of officials and other servants of the European Communities Pursuant to Article 83a of the Staff Regulations, each year the Commission shall present to the Council an updated version of the actuarial assessment of the pension scheme, in accordance with Article 1(2) of Annex XII.
Eurostat has carried out a study of the scheme in accordance with Article 13 of Annex XII to the Staff Regulations which sets the rate of contribution required to maintain actuarial balance at 10.4%.
