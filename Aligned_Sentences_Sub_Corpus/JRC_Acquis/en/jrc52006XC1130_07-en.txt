Revision by France of a public service obligation in respect of scheduled air services between Paris (Orly) and Béziers Revision by France of a public service obligation in respect of scheduled air services between Paris (Orly) and Béziers (2006/C 291/10) 1.
The new public service obligation is as follows: 2.1.
Continuity of service Except in cases of force majeure, the number of flights cancelled for reasons directly attributable to the carrier must not exceed 3 % of the minimum number of required flights in any operating year.
