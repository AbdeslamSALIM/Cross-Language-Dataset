Article 3 Under the criteria listed in Article 1, the following countries and territory shall be withdrawn from the list of countries and territories benefiting from the generalized preferences set out in Annex III to Regulations (EC) No 3281/94 and (EC) No 1256/96: - Hong Kong, - Singapore, - South Korea.
Article 4 This Regulation shall enter into force on 1 May 1998.
