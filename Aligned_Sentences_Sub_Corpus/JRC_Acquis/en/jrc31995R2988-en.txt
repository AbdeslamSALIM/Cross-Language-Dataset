TITLE II Administrative measures and penalties Article 4 1.
Application of the measures referred to in paragraph 1 shall be limited to the withdrawal of the advantage obtained plus, where so provided for, interest which may be determined on a flat-rate basis. 3.
Suspension of the administrative proceedings shall suspend the period of limitation provided for in Article 3. 2.
Paragraphs 1 to 4 shall not apply to financial penalties which form an integral part of financial support systems and may be applied independently of any criminal penalties, if and in so far as they are not equivalent to such penalties.
