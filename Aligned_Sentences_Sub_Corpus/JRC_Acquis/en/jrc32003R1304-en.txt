Article 2 Register of requested opinions and own-initiative opinions The Authority shall establish a register of requested opinions and own-initiative opinions which is accessible to the public.
Article 3 Refusal of requests for opinions 1.
The Authority may refuse a request in the cases described in Article 29(4) and (5) of Regulation (EC) No 178/2002. 3.
If the Commission submits such a request, the provisions of Article 6 of the present Regulation shall apply.
