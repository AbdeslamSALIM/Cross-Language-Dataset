Article 2 Checking systems 1.
Each Member State shall organize checks in such a way that: - they cover each year at least 1 % of days worked by drivers of vehicles falling within the scope of Regulations (EEC) No 3820/85 and (EEC) No 3821/85, - not less than 15 % of the total number of the working days checked shall be checked at the roadside and not less than 25 % at the premises of undertakings. 3.
