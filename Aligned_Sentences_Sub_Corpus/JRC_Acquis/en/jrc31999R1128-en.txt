Import licences under this Regulation may be issued on a multiannual basis for periods from 1 July to 30 June of the following year (hereinafter called %quot%the year of import%quot%), for individual years of import only for 178000 animals falling within CN code 0102 90 05 originating in the countries listed in Annex I.
The quota shall have the serial number 09.4598 2.
For those animals, the ad valorem duty and the specific duties fixed in the Common Customs Tariff (CCT) shall be reduced by 80 %. 3.
The 124600 head shall be allocated on the basis of applications for import rights among the eligible importers in proportion to their imports of animals within the meaning of the first subparagraph of paragraph 3(a) during the period referred to in that point and proven in accordance with paragraph 6. 5.
The 53400 head shall be allocated among the eligible traders in proportion to the quantities applied for within the meaning of paragraph 3(b) and proven in accordance with paragraph 6. 6.
Traders who on 1 June preceding the year of import in question were no longer engaged in any activity in the beef and veal sector shall not qualify for the allocation under the first subparagraph of Article 2(3)(a). 2.
If the reduction referred to in the preceding subparagraph results in a quantity of less than 100 head per application, the allocation shall be made by drawing lots, by batches of 100 head, by the Member States concerned.
If the remaining quantity is less than 100 head, it shall constitute a single batch.
Imports of the quantities allocated in accordance with Article 5 shall be subject to the presentation of one or more import licences. 2.
