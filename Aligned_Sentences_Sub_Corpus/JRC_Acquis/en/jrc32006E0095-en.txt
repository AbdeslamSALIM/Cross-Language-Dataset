Council Common Position 2006/95/CFSP of 14 February 2006 renewing restrictive measures against the leadership of the Transnistrian region of the Republic of Moldova Council Common Position 2006/95/CFSP of 14 February 2006 renewing restrictive measures against the leadership of the Transnistrian region of the Republic of Moldova THE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION, Having regard to the Treaty on European Union, and in particular Article 15 thereof, Whereas: (1) On 23 February 2004, the Council adopted Common Position 2004/179/CFSP [1] concerning restrictive measures, in the form of restrictions on admission, against the leadership of the Transnistrian region of the Republic of Moldova.
Article 2 This Common Position shall take effect on the date of its adoption.
Article 3 This Common Position shall be published in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Done at Brussels, 14 February 2006.
