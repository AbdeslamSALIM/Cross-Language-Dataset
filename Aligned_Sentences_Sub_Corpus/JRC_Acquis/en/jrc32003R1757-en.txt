In addition, fruits must be: - firm, - free of cavities, - free of splits, - sufficiently developed, without lignification of the seeds.
They must be: - well developed, - well shaped, - with their stalk neatly cut and of a maximum length of 3 cm.
For Classes Extra and I, courgettes shall be sized according to the following scale: - 50 g to 100 g included, - 100 g excluded to 225 g included, - 225 g excluded to 450 g.
However, this tolerance is applicable only to produce which differs by not more than 10 per cent from the size or weight limits specified.
