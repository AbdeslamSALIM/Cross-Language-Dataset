Article 2 The conditions for the authorisation of the preparations No 16 and No 17 belonging to the group %quot%Enzymes%quot% listed in Annex II to the present Regulation are hereby replaced by those set out in the said Annex according to Directive 70/524/EEC.
Article 3 The preparations belonging to the group %quot%Enzymes%quot% listed in Annex III to the present Regulation shall be authorised according to Directive 70/524/EEC as additives in animal nutrition under the conditions laid down in the said Annex.
Article 4 The preparation belonging to the group %quot%Micro-organisms%quot% listed in Annex IV to the present Regulation shall be authorised according to Directive 70/524/EEC as additives in animal nutrition under the conditions laid down in the said Annex.
Article 5 This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
