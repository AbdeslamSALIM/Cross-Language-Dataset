Case T-334/05: Action brought on 29 August 2005 — Wineke Neirinck v Commission Action brought on 29 August 2005 — Wineke Neirinck v Commission Parties Applicant(s): Wineke Neirinck (Brussels, Belgium) (represented by: G.
Pleas in law and main arguments The applicant first of all worked in the Commission as a seconded national expert from 1 May 1998 to 30 April 2001, then as a member of the temporary staff under a contract which expired on 30 April 2004.
She alleges that DG ADMIN refused to recruit her on the basis that she had already served the maximum period of six years.
