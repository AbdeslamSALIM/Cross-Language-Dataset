Authorisation for State aid pursuant to Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty — Cases where the Commission raises no objections Authorisation for State aid pursuant to Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty Cases where the Commission raises no objections (2006/C 148/10) Date of adoption of the decision : 15.5.2006 Member State : The Republic of Lithuania Aid No : N 21/2006 Title : Compensation of losses due to the application of phytosanitary measures Objective : Aid for combating plant diseases Legal basis - Lietuvos Respublikos fitosanitarijos įstatymas (Žin. 1999 m., Nr.113-3285); - 2003 m.
