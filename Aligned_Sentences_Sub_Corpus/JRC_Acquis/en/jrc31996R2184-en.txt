Article 2 The arrangements for the implementation of this Regulation, including any surveillance measures, shall be adopted in accordance with the procedure laid down in Article 27 of Regulation (EEC) No 1418/76.
Article 3 Regulation (EEC) No 1250/77 is hereby repealed.
Article 4 This Regulation shall enter into force on the third day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.

Done at Luxembourg, 28 October 1996.
