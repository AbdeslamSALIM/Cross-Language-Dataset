Article 2 This Decision shall enter into force on the date of its adoption.
It shall be applicable from 1 July 2005.
However, the competent authorities may continue to accept transit declarations made in writing until 31 December 2006 at the latest.
Where the competent authorities decide to accept transit declarations made in writing after 1 July 2005, the decision shall be communicated in advance to the Commission in writing.
Done at Bern, 15 August 2005.
