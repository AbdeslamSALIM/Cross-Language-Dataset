Article 2 Fishery products imported into the Community from the United Arab Emirates shall comply with Articles 3, 4 and 5.
Article 4 The fishery products shall come from approved establishments, factory vessels, or cold stores, or from registered freezer vessels listed in Annex II.
Article 5 All packages shall bear the words %quot%UNITED ARAB EMIRATES%quot% and the approval/registration number of the establishment, factory vessel, cold store or freezer vessel of origin in indelible letters, except in the case of frozen fishery products in bulk and intended for the manufacture of preserved foods.
Article 6 This Decision shall apply from 8 December 2003.
Article 7 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 15 October 2003.
