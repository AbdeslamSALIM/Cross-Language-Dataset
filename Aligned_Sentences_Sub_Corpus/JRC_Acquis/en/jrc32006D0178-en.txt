2006/178/EC: Commission Decision of 27 February 2006 setting up a High Level Expert Group on Digital Libraries (This text annuls and replaces the text published in Official Journal L 46 of 16 February 2006, p. 32 ) Commission Decision of 27 February 2006 setting up a High Level Expert Group on Digital Libraries (This text annuls and replaces the text published in Official Journal L 46 of 16 February 2006, p. 32) (2006/178/EC) THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, Having regard to the Treaty establishing the European Community, Whereas: (1) Article 157 of the Treaty establishing the European Community assigns the Community and the Member States the task of ensuring that the conditions necessary for the competitiveness of the Community's industry exist.
Article 2 Task The Commission may consult the group on any matter relating to the implementation of the digital libraries initiative as set out in the Communication.
Article 3 Composition — Appointment 1.
Article 4 Operation 1.
Article 5 Meeting expenses The Commission may reimburse travel and subsistence expenses for members, experts and observers in connection with the group’s activities in accordance with the provisions in force at the Commission.
Article 6 Entry into force The decision shall take effect on the day of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
It is applicable until 31 December 2008.
