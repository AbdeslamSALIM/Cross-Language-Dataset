Where goods falling within subheadings 1902 11 00 and 1902 19 of the combined nomenclature are exported to a destination other than the United States of America, the special refund fixed for the export of cereals in the form of goods falling within subheadings 1902 11 00 and 1902 19 of the combined nomenclature to the United States of America shall not be taken into consideration: - for determining the lowest rate of refund within the meaning of Article 21 of Regulation (EEC) No 2730/79, - for the purposes of Articles 4 (7) and 5 (3) of Regulation (EEC) No 565/80. 2.
Done at Brussels, 22 December 1987.
