Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3983 — Polestar/Prisa/Ibersuizas /Iberian Capital/Dédalo) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.3983 — Polestar/Prisa/Ibersuizas /Iberian Capital/Dédalo) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2005/C 275/02) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 28 October 2005, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Iberian Capital Fund (GP) Limited (%quot%Iberian Capital%quot%, Spain) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of Dédalo Grupo Gráfico S.L. (%quot%Dédalo%quot%, Spain) by way of acquisition of shares.
Dédalo is presently controlled by Prisaprint S.L. (%quot%Prisaprint%quot%, Spain), Polestar–Watmoughs Netherlands B.V. (%quot%Polestar%quot%, the Netherlands) and Inversiones Ibersuizas S.A. (%quot%Ibersuizas%quot%, Spain); following the transaction these three companies will keep joint control, with Iberian Capital, over Dédalo. 2.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.3983 — Polestar/Prisa/Ibersuizas/Iberian Capital/Dédalo, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 BE-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. [2] OJ C 56, 5.3.2005, p. 32. --------------------------------------------------
