These obligations replace those published in Official Journal of the European Communities C 72 of 6 March 2001. 2.
The public service obligations in respect of scheduled air services between Angers (Marcé) and Lyon (Saint-Exupéry) and between Tours (Val de Loire) and Lyon (Saint-Exupéry) are as follows: Minimum frequency The minimum level of service provided must be two return trips a day, morning and evening, excluding public holidays, from Monday to Friday, 220 days a year.
The route must be as follows: Angers (Marcé) — Tours (Val de Loire) — Lyon (Saint-Exupéry) and vice versa.
Continuity of service: Except in cases of force majeure, the number of flights cancelled for reasons directly attributable to the carrier must not exceed 3 % of the number of flights scheduled in any year.
