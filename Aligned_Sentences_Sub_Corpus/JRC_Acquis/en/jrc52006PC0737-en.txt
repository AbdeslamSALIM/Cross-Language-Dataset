Proposal for a Council Regulation amending Regulation (EEC) No 3030/93 on common rules for imports of certain textile products from third countries /* COM/2006/0737 final - ACC 2006/0242 */ [pic] | COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES | Brussels, 28.11.2006 COM(2006)737 final 2006/0242 (ACC) Proposal for a COUNCIL REGULATION amending Regulation (EEC) No 3030/93 on common rules for imports of certain textile products from third countries (presented by the Commission) EXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM CONTEXT OF THE PROPOSAL | 110 | Grounds for and objectives of the proposal As from 1 January 2007, the European Union will include 2 new Member States, Romania and Bulgaria.
This necessitates amendments to Council Regulation (EEC) No 3030/93. | 120 | General context In order to prevent that the enlargement of the Community would have restrictive effects on trade in an enlarged EU, it is appropriate to adjust the quota levels.
The methodology used takes into account the traditional imports into the new Member States, as the EU did for the previous enlargements. | 130 | Existing provisions in the area of the proposal OJ L 79, 17.3.2004, p. 1, OJ L 177, 8.7.2005, p. 19, OJ L 7, 12.1.2006, p. 8. | CONSULTATION OF INTERESTED PARTIES AND IMPACT ASSESSMENT | Consultation of interested parties | 219 | No formal consultation is necessary within the enlargement process. | Collection and use of expertise | 229 | There was no need for external expertise. | 230 | Impact assessment No impact assessment needed as Regulation is direct consequence of Enlargement decision. | LEGAL ELEMENTS OF THE PROPOSAL | 305 | Summary of the proposed action The proposal is an amendment of Council Regulation (EEC) No 3030/93 on common rules for imports of certain textile products from third countries.
