Notice inviting applications for authorisation to prospect for hydrocarbons in block segment L16b of the Dutch continental shelf (2005/C 107/06) (Text with EEA relevance) The Minister for Economic Affairs of the Kingdom of the Netherlands serves notice that an application for authorisation to prospect for hydrocarbons has been received for the segment of block L16 indicated on the map appended as Annex 3 to the Mining Regulation (Government Gazette 2002, 245), known as "block segment L16b".
