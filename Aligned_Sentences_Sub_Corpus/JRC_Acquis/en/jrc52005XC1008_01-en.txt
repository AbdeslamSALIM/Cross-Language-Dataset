This document has been prepared in cooperation with experts representing: (1) the authorities concerned in the Member States, (2) companies in the "European Fertilizer Manufacturers' Association" (EFMA), (3) the European Committee for Standardization (CEN) TC 260. 1.
This information file is the result of the implementation of Article 14 of the Regulation (EC) No 2003/2003 which stipulates that: (a) it provides nutrients in an effective manner; (b) relevant sampling, analysis, and if required, test methods are being provided. (c) under normal conditions of use it does not adversely affect human, animal, or plant health, or the environment.
CONTENTS OF THE TECHNICAL FILE The file must contain at least five separate chapters: - information on effects on health, environment and safety, - agronomic data, - details of the methods of analysis and the results, - a proposal for inclusion in Annex I to Regulation (EC) No 2003/2003, - any other relevant information. 3.
