Article 2 The products set out in Annex II shall have their conformity attested by a procedure whereby, in addition to a factory-production control system operated by the manufacturer, an approved certification body is involved in assessment and surveillance of the production control or of the product itself.
Article 3 The procedure for attesting conformity as set out in Annex III shall be indicated in mandates for guidelines for European technical approvals.
Article 4 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
