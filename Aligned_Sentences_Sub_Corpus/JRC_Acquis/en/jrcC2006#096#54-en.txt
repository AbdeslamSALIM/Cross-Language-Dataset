Case F-6/06: Action brought on 13 January 2006 — Nicola Scafarto v Commission Action brought on 13 January 2006 — Nicola Scafarto v Commission Parties Applicant: Nicola Scafarto (Luxembourg, Luxembourg) (represented by: A.
By his application he claims primarily that the decision determining his grade infringes Article 31 of the Staff Regulations.
He goes on to submit that, in any event, that decision is unlawful, in so far as its legal basis, Article 12 of Annex XIII to the Staff Regulations, is unlawful on the ground that it infringes the following principles: legal certainty, the protection of legitimate expectations, non-discrimination, equal treatment, reasonableness and proper administration.
