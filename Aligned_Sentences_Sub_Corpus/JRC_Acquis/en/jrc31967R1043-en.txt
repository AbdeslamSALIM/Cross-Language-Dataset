Article 2 For the purposes of calculating the average annual sugar production of a factory or undertaking referred to in Article 23 or Regulation No 1009/67/EEC: - production of white sugar shall be taken into account, disregarding differences in quality; (1) OJ No 308, 18.12.1967, p. 1. (2) OJ No 313, 22.12.1967, p. 2. - production of raw sugar shall be expressed in terms of white sugar; - production of liquid sugar shall be expressed in terms of white sugar.
Article 4 The basic quotas allocated shall be expressed in terms of white sugar.
Article 5 This Regulation shall enter into force on the third day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
