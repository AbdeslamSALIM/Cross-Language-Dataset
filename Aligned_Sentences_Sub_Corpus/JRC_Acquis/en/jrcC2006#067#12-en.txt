Initiation of proceedings (Case COMP/M.4009 — CIMC/BURG) Text with EEA relevance Initiation of proceedings (Case COMP/M.4009 — CIMC/BURG) (2006/C 67/12) (Text with EEA relevance) On 13 March 2006, the Commission decided to initiate proceedings in the above-mentioned case after finding that the notified concentration raises serious doubts as to its compatibility with the common market.
