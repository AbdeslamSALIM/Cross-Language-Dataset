Checks on compliance with the deadlines for the purposes of the payment of advances against booking of expenditure shall be made twice each financial year: - on expenditure effected by 31 March, - on expenditure effected by 31 July.
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on the seventh day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
Done at Brussels, 1 August 2001.
