Article 4 The country of origin shall be indicated in Section 14 of the application and of the import licence.
Only a party to a multiplication contract who is established in the Community may qualify for the provisions of Article 5 (2).
The application and the licence itself shall include in Section 12 one of the following endorsements: - Import under a multiplication contract, - Indførsel foretaget inden for rammerne af en formeringskontrakt, - Im Rahmen eines Vertrages über vermehrtes Saatgut getätigte Einfuhr, - Importation réalisée dans le cadre d'un contrat de multiplication, - Importazione effettuata nell'ambito di un contratto di moltiplicazione, - Invoer in het kader van een vermeerderingscontract. 3.
