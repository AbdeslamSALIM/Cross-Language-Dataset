Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4344 — Lactalis/Nestlé) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4344 — Lactalis/Nestlé) (2006/C 199/02) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 14 August 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertaking Groupe Lactalis S.A. (Lactalis, France) and the undertaking Nestlé S.A. (Nestlé, Switzerland) create, within the meaning of Articles 3(4) and 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation, a joint venture (hereafter, %quot%the Undertaking%quot%) by way of transfer of assets. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Lactalis: food company, - for Nestlé: food company, - for the Undertaking: dairy products. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4344 — Lactalis/Nestlé, to the ollowing address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. --------------------------------------------------
