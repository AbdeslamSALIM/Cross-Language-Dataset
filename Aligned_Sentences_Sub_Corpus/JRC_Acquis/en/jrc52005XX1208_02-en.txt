Notification by the Kingdom of Norway concerning visa reciprocity Notification by the Kingdom of Norway concerning visa reciprocity [1] (2005/C 310/02) In accordance with Article 2 of Council Regulation (EC) No 851/2005 of 2 June 2005 amending Regulation (EC) No 539/2001, I wish to inform you that as of today nationals of the Kingdom of Norway are subject to a visa requirement in the following third countries listed in Annex II to Regulation (EC) No 539/2001: - Australia - Brunei (visa required for stays exceeding 14 days) - Panama (visa issued free of charge on arrival). [1] This notification is published in conformity with Article 2 of Council Regulation (EC) No 851/2005 of 2 June 2005 (OJ L 141, 4.6.2005, p. 3) amending Regulation (EC) No 539/2001 of 15 March 2001 (OJ L 81, 21.3.2001, p. 1). --------------------------------------------------
