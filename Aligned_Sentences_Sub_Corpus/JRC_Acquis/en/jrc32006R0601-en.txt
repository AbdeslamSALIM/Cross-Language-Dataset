Article 2 Data format Member States shall use the data format "Gesmes", in conformity with the interchange standards specified by the Commission (Eurostat).
Article 3 Technical specifications of data format The technical specifications of the data structure shall be as given in the Annex to this Regulation.
Article 4 Entry into force This Regulation shall enter into force on the 20th day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
