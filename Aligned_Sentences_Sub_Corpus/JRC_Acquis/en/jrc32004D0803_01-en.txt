Article 2 The EU Operation Commander is hereby authorised with immediate effect to release the activation order (ACTORD) in order to execute the deployment of the forces, prior to Transfer of Authority following their arrival in theatre, and start execution of the mission on 2 December 2004.
Article 3 Without prejudice to Article 17 of Joint Action 2004/570/CFSP, this Decision shall remain in force until the Council decides to end the EU military operation in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
Article 4 This Decision shall enter into force on the date of its adoption.
Article 5 This Decision shall be published in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Done at Brussels, 25 November 2004.
