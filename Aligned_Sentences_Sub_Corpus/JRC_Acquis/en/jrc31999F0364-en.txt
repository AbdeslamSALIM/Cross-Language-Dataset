Article 2 In the consultations on the Convention, Member States shall, as far as is practicable, coordinate their positions, at the Presidency's initiative, and seek to arrive at common standpoints on all issues which have significant implications for the interests of the European Union.
Article 3 The Council shall endeavour to achieve further common positions, as necessary, in relation to the Convention.
Article 4 This Common Position shall be published in the Official Journal.
Done at Brussels, 27 May 1999.
