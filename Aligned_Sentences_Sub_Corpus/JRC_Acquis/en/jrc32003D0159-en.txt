Where, at the initiative of the Commission or a Member State, the Council considers that an ACP State has failed to fulfil an obligation concerning one of the essential elements referred to in Article 9 of the Partnership Agreement, or in serious cases of corruption, the ACP State concerned shall be invited, unless there is special urgency, to hold consultations in accordance with Articles 96 and 97 of the Partnership Agreement.
The European Parliament shall be immediately and fully informed on any decision taken under paragraphs 1 and 2.
Article 4 This Decision shall be published in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Done at Brussels, 19 December 2002.
