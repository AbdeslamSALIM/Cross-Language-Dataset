Flights carried out using aircraft with a minimum capacity of 120 seats must offer adequate guarantees for the transport of persons with disabilities (WCHR, WCHS, WCHC) and of stretchers, coffins and, if necessary, goods.
Sums received in this connection will be allocated to the territorial continuity heading of the budget of Sicily; - guarantee that flights are operated to within 30 minutes of the established timetable (punctuality index); - compensate each passenger for any delay of more than 30 minutes by giving a credit of EUR 15,00 to be used in the purchase of a subsequent ticket.
