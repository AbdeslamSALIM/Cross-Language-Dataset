Article 2 The Commission commits itself to creating a gender balance in expert groups and committees established by it.
The aim in the medium term is to reach at least 40 % of members of one sex in each expert group and committee.
Article 3 Three years after the adoption of the present Decision the Commission shall review its implementation and publish a report, which shall include statistical analysis of the gender balance in expert groups and committees.
Done at Brussels, 19 June 2000.
