Article 2 The President of the Council is hereby authorised to designate the person(s) empowered to sign the Agreement and related documents on behalf of the European Union, subject to conclusion.
Article 3 This Decision shall apply to the fields covered by the provisions listed in Annexes A and B to the Agreement and to their development to the extent that such provisions have a legal base within the Treaty on European Union or to the extent that it has been determined under Decision 1999/436/EC [5] that they have such a base.
The provisions of Articles 1 to 4 of Decision 1999/437/EC shall apply, in the same way, to the association of Switzerland with the implementation, application and development of the Schengen acquis, which falls under Title VI of the Treaty on European Union. 2.
Done at Brussels, 25 October 2004.
