Spain has prohibited fishing for this stock from 3 November 2004.
This date should be adopted in this Regulation also, HAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION: Article 1 Catches of black scabbardfish in the waters of ICES sub-areas V, VI, VII and XII (Community waters and waters not under the sovereignty or jurisdiction of third countries) by vessels flying the flag of Spain or registered in Spain are hereby deemed to have exhausted the quota allocated to Spain for 2004.
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
It shall apply from 3 November 2004.
Done at Brussels, 30 November 2004.
