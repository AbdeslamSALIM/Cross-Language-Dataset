Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4219 — ABN Amro/Nextiraone Europe) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4219 — ABN Amro/Nextiraone Europe) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 113/05) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 2 May 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertakings ABN Amro Capital France and ABN Amro Capital BO Funds BV, both belonging to the group ABN (%quot%ABN Amro%quot%, Netherlands) acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking NextiraOne Europe Holdings B.V. (%quot%Nextiraone Europe%quot%, Netherlands) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for ABN Amro: Financial services, private equity and asset management, - for Nextiraone Europe: telecommunications solutions for corporate customers. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4219 — ABN Amro/Nextiraone Europe, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition, Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Brussels [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. [2] OJ C 56, 5.3.2005, p. 32. --------------------------------------------------
