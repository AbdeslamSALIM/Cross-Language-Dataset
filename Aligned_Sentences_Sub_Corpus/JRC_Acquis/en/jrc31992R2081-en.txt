Article 15 The Commission shall be assisted by a committee composed of the representatives of the Member States and chaired by the representative of the Commission.
The opinion shall be delivered by the majority laid down in Article 148 (2) of the Treaty in the case of decisions which the Council is required to adopt on a proposal from the Commission.
Article 16 Detailed rules for applying this Regulation shall be adopted in accordance with the procedure laid down in Article 15.
