The flat-rate aid per hectare for continuing to cultivate vines for the production of quality wines psr provided for in Article 9 of Regulation (EC) No 2019/93 shall be granted at the request of wine producer groups or organisations for areas planted with vine varieties suitable for the production of quality wines psr. 2.
The producer organisations shall be those referred to in Article 39 of Regulation (EC) No 1493/1999.
However, for the 2002/03 wine year, applications shall be lodged no later than 31 December 2002. 2.
Article 5 The competent Greek authority shall notify the Commission by 30 April of the areas for which aid applications have been made and in respect of which aid has actually been paid.
On the basis of a risk analysis, the national authorities shall carry out random on-the-spot checks of a number of aid applications representing at least 5 % of the assisted areas. 6.
Article 8 of Council Regulation (EC) No 1258/1999(4) on the financing of the common agricultural policy shall apply.
Article 7 Regulation (EEC) No 3112/93 is hereby repealed.
Article 8 This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities.
Done at Brussels, 8 November 2002.
