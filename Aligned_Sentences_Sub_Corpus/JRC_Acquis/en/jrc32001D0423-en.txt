Article 2 Periodicity The periodicity of publication or dissemination shall be comparable to that of the results transmitted.
Article 3 Confidentiality The dissemination or publication of statistical data collected under Directive 95/64/EC shall conform to the provisions laid down in Regulation (EEC, Euratom) No 1588/90 and Regulation (EC) No 322/97.
Article 4 Level of detail of disseminated data Until the Commission adopts another decision pursuant to the procedure provided in Article 13 of Directive 95/64/EC, the highest level of detail in which data may be published or disseminated is the level of port to and from maritime coastal area.
Article 5 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 22 May 2001.
