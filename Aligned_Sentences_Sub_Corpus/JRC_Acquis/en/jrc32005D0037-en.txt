Article 2 The ETSC shall analyse and classify every new type of counterfeit euro coin in line with the provisions of Article 5 of Regulation (EC) 1338/2001.
It contributes to the fulfilment of the objectives of the Pericles programme pursuant to Article 4 of Decision 2001/923/EC.
Article 3 The principles on which the organisation of the ETSC will be based are the following: - The Commission may second members of its personnel to the French Mint in order to use its facilities for the analysis of coins. - In accomplishing its functions, the ETSC will be able to use the personnel and equipment of the French Coin National Analysis Centre and the laboratory of the French Mint in Pessac.
Article 4 The Commission shall coordinate the necessary actions to protect euro coins against counterfeiting through periodic meetings of counterfeit coin experts.
Done at Brussels, 29 October 2004.
