Article 2 Member States shall ensure that for the importation of processed feathers or parts of feathers, a commercial document stating that the processed feathers or parts thereof have been treated with a steam current or by some other method ensuring that no pathogens are transmitted accompany the consignment.
Article 3 Member States shall immediately take the necessary measures to comply with this Decision and publish those measures.
Article 4 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
