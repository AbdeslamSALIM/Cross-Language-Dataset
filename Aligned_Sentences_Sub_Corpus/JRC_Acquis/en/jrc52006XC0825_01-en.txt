Specification (summary of requirements under Article 4(2)) Name : "Pohořelický kapr" Description : "Pohořelický kapr" is distinguished by its excellent growth capacity and minimum slaughter yield of 58 %.
Link : Fish farming in the Pohořelice district and Lednice-Valtice area has its roots in the 12th and 13th centuries.
A written reference from 1396 refers to the work carried out on the Měnín (also known as Nesyt) fishpond by Margrave Jošt.
The largest ponds include Starý (around 1520), Lenovický (or Novoveský, 1536) and Vrkoč (1552).
