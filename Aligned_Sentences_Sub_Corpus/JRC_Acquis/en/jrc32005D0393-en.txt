Article 3 Exemption from the exit ban for domestic movements 1.
Domestic movements of animals, their sperm, ova and embryos, from a restricted zone shall be exempted from the exit ban provided that the animals, their sperm, ova and embryos comply with the conditions set out in Annex II or that they comply with paragraphs 2 or 3 of this Article. 2.
Article 5 Exemption from the exit ban for animals leaving the restricted zones for intra-Community trade 1.
When during the transit through a restricted zone, a rest period is foreseen in a staging post, an insecticide treatment shall be carried out in order to protect animals from any attack by vectors. 2.

Article 9 Applicability This Decision shall apply from 13 June 2005.
Article 10 Addressees This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
