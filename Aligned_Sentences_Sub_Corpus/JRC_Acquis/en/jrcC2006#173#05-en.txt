On 14 July 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertakings Mibau Holding GmbH (%quot%Mibau%quot%, Germany) controlled by HeidelbergCement group and Mr.
Hans-Jürgen Hartmann and Foster Yeoman Baumineralien GmbH (%quot%FY%quot%, Germany) belonging to Foster Yeoman group acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of the undertaking Figdor Baustoffhandel GmbH (%quot%Figdor%quot%, Germany) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
