2006/544/EC: Council Decision of 18 July 2006 on guidelines for the employment policies of the Member States Council Decision of 18 July 2006 on guidelines for the employment policies of the Member States (2006/544/EC) THE COUNCIL OF THE EUROPEAN UNION, Having regard to the Treaty establishing the European Community, and in particular Article 128(2) thereof, Having regard to the proposal from the Commission, Having regard to the opinion of the European Parliament [1], Having regard to the opinion of the European Economic and Social Committee [2], Having consulted the Committee of the Regions, Having regard to the opinion of the Employment Committee [3], Whereas: (1) The reform of the Lisbon Strategy in 2005 has placed the emphasis on growth and jobs.
Article 2 This Decision is addressed to the Member States.
Done at Brussels, 18 July 2006.
