Dragonflies and butterflies fluttering around restless, and wasps and bees buzzed looking for honey.
Sparrows, goldfinches, swallows and a hundred other species of birds painted and cheerful go to the countryside at dawn to catch seeds, grasshoppers and other insects that feed; but all nesting Villalegre term, and return to him after his excursions to take shelter in their preserves and dark, drinking in their crystal streams and ditches, and to rejoice that oasis with chirps, trills and gurgles
