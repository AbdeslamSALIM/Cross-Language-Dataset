after a remarkable brave new world marked the return of bruce dickinson and adrian smith, i expect the cataclysm, the masterpiece.
this is the iron maiden, undeniably, the sound and style of compounds attested but i think it is sorely lacking inspiration.
the group has a well proven recipe and has succeeded in recent years to offer albums admittedly unoriginal but highly effective.
one finds here and there a few sparks of the past, but the flame has disappeared ...
until the next.
