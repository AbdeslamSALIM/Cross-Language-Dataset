      Waste (vote)
    
    
      - Before the vote on Amendment 183
    
    
      
        A Uachtaráin, ar an drochuair bhí meancóg cló ag 183 nuair a chuireamar isteach é agus ní fhacamar é go dtí an bomaite deireanach.
        In ionad na dátaí 2012 - 2015 ba chóir go mbeadh na dátaí 2009 - 2012, in (a) agus (b).
        Tá mé buartha faoin mheancóg.
      
      
        I am sorry, there was a tabling error here.
        The Tabling Office is aware of it.
        Instead of the dates '2012 - 2015' there should have been the dates '2009 - 2012' in (a) and (b).
      
      
        Mar sin de ba mhaith liom leasú béil a mholadh faoin mheancóg cló a leasú in 183.
      
      
        - The oral amendment was accepted.
