The lefff is a freely available dictionary4 that has been automatically compiled by drawing from different sources and that has been manually validated. We used a version with 553,138 lexical entries including 26,311 multiword ones (22,673 multiword nouns). Their different modes of acquisition makes those two resources complementary. In both, lexical entries are composed of a inflected form, a lemma, a part-of-speech and morphological features.
