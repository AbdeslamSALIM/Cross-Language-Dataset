 Luxembourg was one of the countries to participate in the first Eurovision Song Contest in 1956. It has also been one of the most successful, having won the contest a total of 5 times. Luxembourg quit the contest in 1994 after a run of bad placing in the late 80s and early 90s.In 2004, RTL were to return to Eurovision after an absence of 11 years. However, after monetary issues with, Luxembourg withdrew. Strong rumours indicated that RTL would participate at the 2005 contest. However, RTL later decided against it, and said that they would "never" return.In recent years the Luxembourg branch of OGAE has been campaigning for Luxembourg to return to the contest.
