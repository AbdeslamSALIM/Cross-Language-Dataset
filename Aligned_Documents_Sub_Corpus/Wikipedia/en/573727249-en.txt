Despite being only 172&nbsp;cm tall, he is a good header of the ball, good jumper and often scores goals with his head. Cordoba is also a versatile defender. He sometimes plays as right back and has even filled the left back position.He captained the Colombia national football team to win the  competition, scoring the only goal in the final.
