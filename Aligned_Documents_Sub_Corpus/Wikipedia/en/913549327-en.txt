 |composer = Alexandru Cristea |author = Alexei Mateevici |adopted = 1994 |until =  Limba noastră ("Our Language") has been since 1994 the national anthem of the Republic of Moldova. For a short period before that, its official anthem was Deşteaptă-te, române!, which is also the national anthem of Romania. Albeit being in Moldovan/Romanian, the poem makes no reference to the Romanian or Moldovan designation. It allegedly makes reference to the previously used Romanian national anthem when referring to a "Sleep of Death".The lyrics are from Alexei Mateevici (1888-1917) and the music was composed by Alexandru Cristea (1890-1942). The original poem contains twelve stanzas, five of which have been kept for the anthem.
