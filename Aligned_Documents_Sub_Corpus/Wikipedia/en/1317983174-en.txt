 mapx = 44.5333 | Rezzoaglio is a comune (municipality) in the province of Genoa in the Italian region Liguria. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,179 and an area of 104.9 km².All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.Rezzoaglio borders the following municipalities: Borzonasca, Favale di Malvaro, Ferriere, Fontanigorda, Lorsica, Montebruno, Orero, Ottone, Rovegno, San Colombano Certénoli, Santo Stefano d'Aveto.GeographyRezzoaglio is located about 40 km northeast of Genoa in the Aveto Valley near the Aveto torrent.The municipality of Rezzoaglio contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Alpepiana, Brignole, Brugnoni, Cabanne, Ca' degli Alessandri, Calcinara, Calzagatta, Cardenosa, Casaleggio, Cascine, Cerisola, Villa Cerro, Chiappetta, Codorso, Cognoli, Costafigara, Costigliolo, Ertola, Esola, Farfanosa, Garba, Ghierto, Gragnolosa, Groparolo, Isola, Isolarotonda, Isoletta, Lovari, Magnasco, Mandriole, Mileto, Moglia, Molini, Monte, Noci, Parazzuolo, Pianazze, Piandifontana, Piandomestico, Villa Piano, Prato della Casa, Priosa, Rezzoaglio Inferiore, Villa Rocca, Roncopiano, Villa Salto, Sbarbari, Scabbiamara, Segaglia, Tecchia, Ventarola, Vicomezzano, Vicosoprano, Villa Cella, Villa Noce, and Lago delle Lame.Rezzoaglio is part of the Aveto Natural Regional Park.
