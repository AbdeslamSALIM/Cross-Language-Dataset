Estaing is a commune in the Aveyron département in southern France.Located in the North of the Aveyron département in the Midi-Pyrénées, Estaing is considered as one of the most picturesque villages in France, populated by approximately 600 people, the village is very quiet during the winter months, it is in the summer that the village shows the most signs of life. Lying at 320 metres above sea level, the winters are cool and the summers are hot and dry.
