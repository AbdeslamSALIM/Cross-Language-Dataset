 Sassari (, , ) is a province in the autonomous island region of Sardinia in Italy. Its capital is the city of Sassari.It has an area of 4,282 km², and a total population of 322,326 (2001). There are 66 municipalities (comuni) in the province, the largest of which are Sassari (pop. 120,729 in 2001 census), Alghero (38,404), Porto Torres (21,064), Sorso (12,842), and Ozieri (11,334) (source: Italian National Institute of Statistics (Istat), see this link).Points of interestArboreto Mediterraneo del Limbara, an arboretumOrto Botanico dell'Università di Sassari, a new botanical gardenExternal linksOfficial website  Sassari Category:Province of Sassaribg:Сасари (провинция) ar:مقاطعة ساساري ca:Província de Sàsser cs:Provincie Sassari de:Provinz Sassari et:Sassari provints el:Σάσσαρι (επαρχία) es:Provincia de Sassari eo:Provinco de Sassari eu:Sassariko probintzia fr:Province de Sassari id:Provinsi Sassari it:Provincia di Sassari he:סאסארי (נפה) jv:Provinsi Sassari lad:Provinsia de Sassari nl:Sassari (provincie) ja:サッサリ県 no:Provinsen Sassari nn:Provinsen Sassari oc:Província de Sàsser pms:Provincia ëd Sàssari pl:Prowincja Sassari pt:Sassari (província) ro:Provincia Sassari ru:Сассари (провинция) sc:Provìntzia de Tàtari simple:Province of Sassari fi:Sassarin maakunta sv:Sassari (provins) tl:Sassari vi:Sassari (tỉnh) vec:Provincia de Sasari zh:薩薩里省
