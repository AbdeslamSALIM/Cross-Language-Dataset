She was born in Cherbourg, France.She rose to fame when she played against American Serena Williams 6–3, 6–7, 5–7 in this tough match at first round of Australian Open 2003.In her career Loit has won three career singles titles: 2004 Estoril, Casablanca and 2007 Acapulco (all on clay) and eight doubles titles. Emilie's highest WTA Ranking has been #27 in singles and #15 in doubles. However, she has been a shame when she lost to Anastasia Myskina & Vera Zvonareva in penultimate deciding doubles match at FED Cup 2004 with Marion Bartoli, as a result France could not defend their
