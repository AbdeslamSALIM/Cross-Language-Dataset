Marie Luise Alexandrine Karoline (Sigmaringen, 17 November 1845 - Brussels, 26 November 1912) was a Princess of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen, later simply of Hohenzollern, and mother of King Albert I of Belgium.LifeMarie Luise was a daughter of Prince Karl Anton of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen, Prime minister of Prussia and Princess Josephine of Baden.She married on April 25, 1867 in Berlin with Prince Philippe, Count of Flanders, second son of King Leopold I of Belgium and Louise-Marie of Orléans. They had 5 children :Prince Baudouin of Belgium (1869-1891)Princess Henriette, Duchess of Vendôme and Alençon (twin) (1870-1948)Princess Joséphine Marie of Belgium (twin) (1870-1871)Princess Joséphine Caroline of Belgium (1872-1958)Albert I (1875-1934), third King of the Belgians.Marie Luise died in Belgium at the age of 67.
