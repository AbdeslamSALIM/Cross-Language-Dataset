After winning a junior European and world championship in road racing, she continued in senior cycling by becoming world champion in cyclo-cross and road racing at the age of 19. Vos added a track racing world championship when she won the points race in 2008.Early yearsMarianne Vos was born in in 's-Hertogenbosch, Noord-Brabant and lives in the small village of Babyloniënbroek. She started her career when she was six years old after she watched her older brother who was already a cyclist.Persoonlijke gegevens Marianne Vos, mariannevos.nl At first she trained with her brother's team as she was not allowed to participate in races; during the winter she started training in cyclo-cross as well. When she was eight, she was able to ride races.Persoonlijke gegevens Marianne Vos, mariannevos.nl Vos also participated in speed skating and inline speed skating. At 14 she replaced inline skating with mountain biking.Professional career2002, 2003In 2002 she won two national championships and finished second in another.
