The Polish Super Cup in football (official Polish title: "Superpuchar Polski") is an annually held match between the Champion of the (Polish 1st league (Ekstraklasa) and the Polish Cup winner. To this date the Polish SuperCup has been played out 19 times with the Polish Cup winner taking the trophy 11 times, while the Ekstraklasa champions had won the trophy 8 times.
