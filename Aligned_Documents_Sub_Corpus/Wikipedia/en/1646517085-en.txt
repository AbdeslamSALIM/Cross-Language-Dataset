  Al Hudud ash Shamaliyah  (Arabic: منطقة الحدود الشمالية, "The Northern Borders Region") is a province of Saudi Arabia. It is located in the north of the country, bordering Iraq. It has an area of 127,000 km² and a population of 237,100 (1999). Its capital is 'Ar'ar.
