 Cleburne County is a county of the U.S. state of Alabama. Its name is in honor of Major General Patrick Cleburne, of Arkansas. As of 2000 the population was 14,123. Its county seat is Heflin.HistoryCleburne County was established on December 6, 1866, with the County Seat at Edwardsville.
