 Satu Mare County (, , ) is a county (judeţ) of Romania. The capital city is Satu Mare. Besides Romanians (58.8% of the population), Satu Mare features a significant ethnic minority of Hungarians (35.2%).DemographicsIn 2002, it had a population of 367,281 and the population density was 83/km².
