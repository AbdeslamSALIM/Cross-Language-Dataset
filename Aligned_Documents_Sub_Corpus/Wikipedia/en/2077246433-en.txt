Michał was the son of a successful but controversial military commander, Jeremi Michał Wiśniowiecki, known for his ruthless actions against Bohdan Chmielnicki's Uprising.Michał Wiśniowiecki's reign was less than successful. His father's military fame notwithstanding, Michał lost a war against the Turks, who occupied Podole.Poczet.com, Michał Korybut Wisniowiecki. He was unable to cope with his responsibilities and with Poland's quarreling factions.
