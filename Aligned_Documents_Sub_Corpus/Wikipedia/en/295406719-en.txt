 Chelyabinsk Oblast (, Chelyabinskaya oblast) is a federal subject of Russia (an oblast). Its administrative center is the city of Chelyabinsk.Area: 87,900&nbsp;km²; population: 3,603,339 (2002 Census); 3,623,732 (1989 Census).GeographyTime zoneChelyabinsk Oblast is located in the Yekaterinburg Time Zone (YEKT/YEKST). UTC offset is +0500&nbsp;(YEKT)/+0600&nbsp;(YEKST).
