             Hasna Benhassi (; born June 1, 1978 in Marrakesh) is a Moroccan middle distance athlete.At the 2008 Olympics in Beijing, Benhassi finished third winning the bronze medal. At the 2004 Olympics in Athens, she finished second to Kelly Holmes in the women's 800 meters. In August 2005 she won a silver medal in the 800 meters at the 2005 World Championships in Athletics.
