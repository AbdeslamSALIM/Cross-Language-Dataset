The amount of public aid may not exceed 50 % of the total eligible investment expenditure. If the total eligible expenditure called for by the investment in undertakings provided for in the project does not exceed EUR 540000 for one aid recipient in the period from 2004 to 2006, the public financing will be 50 % of that total. The financing breaks down as follows: European Union, 35 %; Republic of Latvia, 15 %; and private, 50 %. The eligible expenditure corresponds to the initial investment defined in Regulation (EC) No 70/2001, as amended: investment in land, buildings, machinery and equipment |
