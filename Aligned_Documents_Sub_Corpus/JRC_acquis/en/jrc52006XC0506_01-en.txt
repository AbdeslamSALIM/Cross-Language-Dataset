Cases where the Commission raises no objections 
(2006/C 107/03) 
(Text with EEA relevance) 
Date of adoption of the decision : 3.5.2005 
Member State : United Kingdom 
Aid No : N 11/2005 
Title : Photovoltaic Demonstration Programme — prolongation until 2006 
Objective : To encourage electricity generation from photovoltaic installations, with the longer term aim of helping meet the UK's requirement for clean electricity supplies. 
Legal basis : The Science and Technology Act 1965 
Budget : GBP 11 million (about EUR 16 million) 
Aid intensity or amount : Maximum 40 % + bonus for SMEs 
Duration : One year prolongation (until March 2006) of the three-year scheme 
Other information : Annual report 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption of the decision : 21.6.2005 
Member State : Poland 
Aid No : N 14/2005 
Title : Regional aid for renewable energy sources related investments 
Objective : To boost renewable energy related investments in the assisted regions 
Legal basis : 
Ustawa o Narodowym Planie Rozwoju; 
Rozporządzenie Ministra Gospodarki i Pracy ws. udzielania pomocy na wspieranie inwestycji związanych z odnawialnymi źródłami energii 
Budget : PLN 653,1 million (about EUR 136,4 million) 
Aid intensity or amount Initial investments : up to 50 % of eligible costs plus 15 percentage points bonus for SMEs; 
Modernisation investments : maximum aid as in case of initial investments plus 10 percentage points bonus or up to 50 % of eligible costs plus 10 percentage points bonus (assisted areas) and 10 percentage points bonus for SMEs. 
Duration : Two year (2005-2006) 
Other information : Annual report 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/
