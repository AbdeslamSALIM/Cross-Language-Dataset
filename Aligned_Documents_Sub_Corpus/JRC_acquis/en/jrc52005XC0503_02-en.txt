Member State: Germany (Sachsen). 
No of the aid: N 633/2003. 
Title: Aid for the costs of transporting and destroying fallen stock. 
Objective: Safe disposal of animal carcasses. SächsAGTierNebG provides that the costs of pick-up, transport, storage, processing and rendering fallen stock within the meaning of Tierseuchengesetz of 11.4.2001 are borne by those having the duty of disposal. Nevertheless, SächsAGTierNebG provides that the owners of animals must pay 25 % of the disposal costs. The disposal costs of carcasses of animals that have been killed within a context of veterinary measures against an animal disease are borne by the municipal districts and cities. 
Legal basis: Sächsisches Ausführungsgesetz zum Tierische Nebenprodukte-Beseitigungsgesetz (SächsAGTierNebG). 
Budget: Approximately EUR 1,5 million per year. 
Aid intensity or amount: 75 % of the costs except 100 % in case of animals killed as a veterinary measure. 
Duration: Until 31 December 2013. 
Other information: The German authorities have specified that one year before the expiration of the contract between the Sachsen authorities and the rendering plant a tendering procedure satisfying the requirements of Community law would be organised for future carcass disposal services.

http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption of the decision: 16 September 2004
