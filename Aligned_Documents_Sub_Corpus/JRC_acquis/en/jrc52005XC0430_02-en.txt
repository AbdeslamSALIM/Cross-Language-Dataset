Member State: Italy (Veneto) 
Aid No: N 289/B/2003 
Title: "Interventi di indennizzo per i pulcini che non hanno potuto essere accasati in conseguenza di influenza aviaria" (compensation for chicks which could not be placed as a result of avian influenza) 
Legal basis: Allegato B) della deliberazione della Giunta regionale del Veneto n. 808 del 28 maggio 2003 di attuazione della legge regionale 7 aprile 2000, n. 8 
Objective: To compensate hatcheries located outside the protection and surveillance zones established by the veterinary authorities for day-old chicks which could not be placed in the holdings that had ordered them because they were located inside those zones. The measure also addressed animal welfare problems as the chicks could not remain in the hatcheries for more than a day after hatching because they did not have the proper structures to raise them (brooding houses). The day-old chicks were therefore put down and incinerated in accordance with the health rules in force 
Budget: The regional authorities plan to allocate a budget of around EUR 800000 
Aid intensity or amount: Up to 80 % of the income lost calculated on the average market values of the day-old chicks in the area concerned during the periods in question 
Duration: Ad hoc measures covering the income lost by poultry-breeding holdings as a result of the epidemics which hit the Veneto region in the periods from the end of August 2000 to the end of March 2001, and from the end of October 2002 to the beginning of October 2003. 
The authentic text(s) of this decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/sg/sgb/state_aids/

No of the aid: N 292/2004. 
Title: Deferred payments of subsidised loans. 
Objective: The notified scheme would make it possible to defer loan payments of subsidised loans by two years at a time or five years in total. For certain types of subsidised loans (state loans) no interest would be paid on the period of deferral. Such deferrals and interest-free periods could be granted on subsidised loans granted for investments, housing or as start-up aid for young farmers pursuant to the national aid scheme, the amendment of which was lastly approved by the Commission with Decision C (2003) 1872. The Finnish authorities have specified that the arrangement does not increase the aid amount or intensity. 
Legal basis: 
Maaseutuelinkeinojen rahoituslaki, valtioneuvoston asetus eräistä maaseudun kehittämiseen myönnettävistä lainoista ja valtioneuvoston asetus maaseudun kehittämisestä, sellaisena kuin se on muutettuna vuonna 2004 
Lagen om finansiering av landsbygdsindustri (Maaseutuelinkeinojen rahoituslaki), regeringens rättsakt om vissa lån som beviljas för landsbygdsutveckling, regeringens rättsakt om landsbygdsutveckling efter ändring 2004. 
Budget: No budgetary impact 
Aid intensity or amount: No changes to the existing aid scheme. 
Duration: Unlimited.
