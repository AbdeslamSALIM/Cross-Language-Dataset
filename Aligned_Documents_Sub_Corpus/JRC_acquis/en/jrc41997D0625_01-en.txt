Decision of the representatives of the governments of the Member States of 2 June 1997 determining the seat of the European Monitoring Centre on Racism and Xenophobia 
DECISION OF THE REPRESENTATIVES OF THE GOVERNMENTS OF THE MEMBER STATES of 2 June 1997 determining the seat of the European Monitoring Centre on Racism and Xenophobia (97/C 194/02) 
THE REPRESENTATIVES OF THE GOVERNMENTS OF THE MEMBER STATES, 
Having regard to Article 17 of Council Regulation (EC) No 1035/97 of 2 June 1997 establishing a European Monitoring Centre on Racism and Xenophobia (1), 
Whereas it is necessary to determine the seat of the European Monitoring Centre on Racism and Xenophobia, 
HAVE DECIDED AS FOLLOWS: 
Article 1 
The European Monitoring Centre on Racism and Xenophobia shall have its seat in Vienna. 
Article 2 
This Decision, which shall be published in the Official Journal ot the European Communities, shall enter into force on the same day as Council Regulation (EC) No 1035/97 of 2 June 1997 establishing a European Monitoring Centre on Racism and Xenophobia. 
Done at Luxembourg, 2 June 1997. 
The President 
Hans VAN MIERLO 
(1) OJ No L 151, 10. 6. 1997, p. 1.
