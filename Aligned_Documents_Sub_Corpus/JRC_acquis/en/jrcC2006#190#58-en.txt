Case F-19/05: Judgment of the Civil Service Tribunal (Second Chamber) of 28 June 2006 — Sanchez Ferriz v Commission (Officials — Career Development Report — Assessment procedure for 2003) 
Judgment of the Civil Service Tribunal (Second Chamber) of 28 June 2006 — Sanchez Ferriz 
v Commission 
(Case F-19/05) [1] 
Parties 
Applicant: Carlos Sanchez Ferriz (Brussels, Belgium) (represented by: G. Bouneou and F. Frabetti, lawyers, then only F. Frabetti) 
Defendant: Commission of the European Communities (represented by: G. Berscheid and L. Lozano Palacios, Agents) 
Re: 
Annulment of the assessment procedure for 2003 in relation to the applicant, or, in the alternative, annulment of the applicant's Career Development Report for 2003 
Operative part of the judgment 
The Tribunal: 
1. Dismisses the action. 
2. Orders each of the parties to bear its own costs. 
[1] OJ C 155 of 25.06.2005 (case initially registered before the Court of First Instance of the European Communities under number T-153/05 and transferred to the European Civil Service Tribunal by order of 15.12.2005).
