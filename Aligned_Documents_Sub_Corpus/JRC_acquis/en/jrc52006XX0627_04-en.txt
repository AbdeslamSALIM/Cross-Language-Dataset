1. A majority of the members of the Advisory Committee agree with the Commission on the basic amount of the fine determined according to the gravity and duration of the infringement in the present case. A minority abstains.

4. The members of the Advisory Committee recommend the publication of its opinion in the Official Journal of the European Union. 
5. The members of the Advisory Committee ask the Commission to take into account all the other points raised during the discussion.
