Judgment of the Court of First Instance of 16 May 2006 — Martin Magone 
v Commission 
(Case T-73/05) [1] 
Parties 
Applicant: Alejandro Martin Magone (Brussels, Belgium) (represented by: É. Boigelot, lawyer)

Re: 
First, an application for annulment of the applicant's career development report for the 2003 assessment exercise, and, second, an application for compensation for material and non-material damage assessed on an equitable basis at EUR 39169,67

1. Dismisses the action. 
2. Orders the parties to bear their own costs.
