Case T-172/06: Action brought on 23 June 2006 — Expasa v OHIM — Juan Gallardo Blanco (figurative mark H ) 
Action brought on 23 June 2006 — Expasa v OHIM — Juan Gallardo Blanco (figurative mark %quot%H%quot%) 
Parties 
Applicant: Expasa, Agricultura y Ganadería, S.A. (Madrid, Spain) (represented by: J.C. Valle Valiente) 
Defendant: Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) 
Other party to the proceedings before the Board of Appeal of OHIM: Juan Gallardo Blanco 
Form of order sought 
- Grant the application; 
- Order OHIM to pay the costs. 
Pleas in law and main arguments 
Registered Community trade mark in respect of which a declaration of invalidity has been sought: Figurative mark %quot%H%quot% (letter written in bold type and with a curve in the horizontal stroke) (application for registration No 1255751, for goods in Class 31). 
Proprietor of the Community trade mark: The applicant. 
Applicant for the declaration of invalidity: Juan Gallardo Blanco. 
Trade mark right of applicant for the declaration: Spanish figurative mark No 198648 %quot%H%quot% (letter written in bold type, slightly inclined, with a curve in the horizontal stroke and crossed by a trail in the colours sky blue and deep red), for goods in Class 31 (%quot%livestock breeding of all kinds, except horses%quot%). 
Decision of the Cancellation Division: Declaration of nullity of the Community trade mark in its entirety. 
Decision of the Board of Appeal: Dismissal of the appeal. 
Pleas in law: Infringement of Articles 40(2) and 74(2) of Regulation (EC) No 40/94 on the Community trade mark.
