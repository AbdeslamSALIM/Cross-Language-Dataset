Maximum aid intensity | The aid regulated by this Order, which is treated as state aid, is in principle deemed to be for general training so that, in accordance with Commission Regulation (EC) No 68/2001 of 12 January 2001, its maximum intensity would be the following where Galicia satisfies the eligibility conditions for regional aid pursuant to Article 87(3)(a) of the Treaty:60 %: large enterprises;80 %: small and medium-sized enterprises.Where, in the light of the applications submitted, some forms of training may be considered to be specific, the intensity would be the following: 35 %: large enterprises;45 %: small and medium-sized enterprises.Where training is provided for disadvantaged workers, the above intensities would be increased by 10 percentage points |
