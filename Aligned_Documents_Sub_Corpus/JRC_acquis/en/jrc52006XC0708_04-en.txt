Annual expenditure planned under the scheme or overall amount of individual aid granted to the company - for 2005: up to SIT 770000000 (EUR 3212350 [1]) 
- for 2006: up to SIT 570000000 (EUR 2377970) 
Total: SIT 1340000000 (EUR 5590320) 
Maximum aid intensity : Maximum amount of aid per applicant is SIT 4000000 (EUR 16687) 
Date of implementation : Implementation will begin in August 2005. 
Duration of scheme or individual aid award : The aid may be granted up to 31 December 2006. 
Objective of the aid : 
In accordance with Article 8, the objective of the aid is to award a grant to partly cover the expenses incurred when next-generation young farmers take over ownership of the family agricultural holding, in line with handover or donation agreements. 
Providing incentives to take over agricultural holdings will improve the situation regarding the ownership and age of Slovenian agricultural structures. The maximum amount of aid is SIT 4000000 per applicant 
Sectors concerned : The scheme is applicable to production in all sectors
