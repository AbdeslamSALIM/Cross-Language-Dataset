Prior notification of a concentration 
(Case COMP/M.4185 — CVCI/Amber Trust II/AB Sanitas) 
Candidate case for simplified procedure 
(2006/C 123/07) 
(Text with EEA relevance) 
1. On 16 May 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertakings Citigroup Venture Capital International Jersey Limited (%quot%CVCI%quot%, Jersey) belonging to the group Citigroup Inc (%quot%Citigroup%quot%, USA) and Amber Trust II SCA (%quot%Amber Trust II%quot%, Luxembourg), jointly controlled by Danske Bank A/S(%quot%Danske Bank%quot%, Denmark) and Firebird Private Equity Advisors LLC (%quot%Firebird%quot%, USA) acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of the undertaking AB Sanitas (%quot%Sanitas%quot%, Lithuania) by way of purchase of shares. 
2. The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: 
- CVCI: private equity investment; 
- Citigroup: global financial services; 
- Amber Trust II: private equity investment; 
- Danske Bank: European financial services company; 
- Firebird: fund management; 
- Sanitas: pharmaceuticals. 
3. On preliminary examination, the Commission finds that the notified transaction could fall within the scope of Regulation (EC) No 139/2004. However, the final decision on this point is reserved. Pursuant to the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [2] it should be noted that this case is a candidate for treatment under the procedure set out in the Notice. 
4. The Commission invites interested third parties to submit their possible observations on the proposed operation to the Commission. 
Observations must reach the Commission not later than 10 days following the date of this publication. Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4185 — CVCI/Amber Trust II/AB Sanitas, to the following address:
