Case T-5/06: Order of the Court of First Instance of 27 June 2006 — Kingdom of Denmark v Commission of the European Communities (Declining of jurisdiction) 
Order of the Court of First Instance of 27 June 2006 — Kingdom of Denmark 
v Commission of the European Communities 
(Case T-5/06) [1] 
Parties 
Applicant: Kingdom of Denmark (represented by: J. Molde, acting as Agent) 
Defendant: Commission of the European Communities (represented by: M. Konstantinidis and H Støvlbaek, acting as Agents) 
Re: 
Application for partial annulment of Commission Decision 2005/717/EC of 13 October 2005 amending for the purposes of adapting to the technical progress the Annex to Directive 2002/95/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council on the restriction of the use of certain hazardous substances in electrical and electronic equipment (OJ 2005 L 271, p. 48). 
Operative part of the order 
1. The Court of First Instance declines jurisdiction in Case T-5/06 Denmark v Commission so that the Court of Justice may rule on the request for annulment. 
2. The costs are reserved. 
[1] OJ C 74, 25.3.2006.
