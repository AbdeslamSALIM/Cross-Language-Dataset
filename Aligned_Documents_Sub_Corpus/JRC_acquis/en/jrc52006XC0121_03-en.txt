Summary information communicated by Member States regarding State aid granted under Commission Regulation (EC) No 1/2004 of 23 December 2003 on the application of Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty to State aid to small and medium-sized enterprises active in the production, processing and marketing of agricultural products 
(2006/C 16/04) 
Aid No : XA 57/05 
Member State : Finland 
Region : Continental Finland, area falling outside the Objective 1 programme 
Title of aid scheme : Aid for a training project 
Legal basis : Valtioneuvoston asetus maaseudun kehittämisestä (609/2000, myöhemmin tehtyine muutoksineen), 52 pykälän 2 momentin 4 kohta 
Annual expenditure planned under the scheme : It is estimated that EUR 1,9 million will be granted to the training project in 2006 
Maximum aid intensity : 
Aid for the training project can be granted for up to 90 % of the eligible costs, but a beneficiary can receive no more than EUR 100000 in any three year period, however. When calculating the aid the beneficiary is deemed to be the person in actual receipt of the technical assistance. 
The aid will be awarded in the form of a grant 
Date of implementation : From 1 January 2006 
Duration of scheme or individual aid award : Aid for the training scheme will be granted in 2006 
Objective of aid : The aim of the training aid is to improve the working practices and skills of farmers and agricultural workers by supporting training. The aid is granted under Article 14 of Commission Regulation (EC) No 1/2004; section 14 of the Community guidelines on State aid to agriculture will be applied when granting the aid 
Economic sector(s) concerned : Agriculture
