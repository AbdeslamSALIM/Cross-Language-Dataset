Date of adoption of the decision : 14.10.2004 
Aid No : N 99/2004 
Member State : Italy 
Title : Tax incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises which collaborate on innovative IT projects (Tecno-Tremonti Law) 
Legal basis : Articolo 1, comma 1, lettera a), seconda frase della legge 326/2003, che ha convertito il decreto legge 269/2003 
Objective : Innovative IT projects to be undertaken by groupings of more than 10 small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) 
Budget : Estimated at EUR 208 million 
Aid intensity or amount : The maximum net intensity is 13 % and the maximum gross intensity 17 % 
Duration : 2004 
Other information : Commitment by the Member State to determine and control eligibility for the aid in question in accordance with the Community framework for state aid for research and development
