Date of adoption of the decision : 13.5.2003 
Member State : Germany 
Aid No : N 147/2003 
Title : R%amp%D project "Integration von selbstkonfigurierenden Konstruktionsstandards in CAD-Systeme", by Kvaerner Warnow Werft GmbH 
Objective : Speed-up the design process by standardisation of design elements of the shipbuilding structures and by providing methods for efficient use of these elements in CAD-systems in the form of data and method banks. In a second step, the partner shipyards will implement a jointly created data and method banks of self-configuring structure specifications which will enable a more efficient collaboration between undertakings involved in the construction of new ships in vertical and horizontal co-operation networks. (Shipbuilding) 
Legal basis : Scheme "Schifffahrt und Meerestechnik für das 21. Jahrhundert" (N156/2000) approved by the Commission on 15 November, 2000 
Budget : EUR 115229 
Aid intensity or amount : 50 % 
Duration : 36 months
