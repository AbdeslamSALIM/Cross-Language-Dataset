Authorisation for State aid pursuant to Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty 
Cases where the Commission raises no objections 
(2006/C 90/05) 
Date of adoption of the decision : 8.3.2006 
Member State : Italy 
Aid No : NN 54 B/2004 
Title : Assistance for the capitalisation of undertakings. Legislative Decree No 102/2004, Article 17 
Objective : Guarantees. The guarantee scheme has been conceived in such a way that it may be considered to involve no state aid within the meaning of Article 87(1) of the Treaty: the conditions set out in point 4.3 of the Commission Notice on the application of Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty to State aid in the form of guarantees have been fulfilled 
Legal basis : Decreto legislativo 29 marzo 2004, n. 102, articolo 17 e Progetto di decreto del Ministero delle politiche agricole e forestali 
Budget : EUR 60 million 
Aid intensity or amount : The measure involves no state aid 
Duration : 6 years 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption of the decision : 20.2.2006

Member State : Germany (Hessen) 
Aid No : N 348/2005 
Title : Agro-Marketing Hessen — Amendment with the Product Group Eggs 
Objective : The quality scheme for promotion and advertising (N 260/A/02) should be extended to eggs 
Legal basis : Hessischer Agrarmarketing-Vertrag

Aid intensity or amount : 50 % for advertising measures, 100 % for participation in trade fairs and exhibitions, 100 % for training measures and consultancy

Member State : Italy (Calabria) 
Aid No : N 372/2004 
Title : Assistance in agricultural areas which have suffered damage (frosts in January/February 2004 in the provinces of Cosenza and Catanzaro, strong winds on 16 April 2004 in the province of Reggio Calabria) 
Objective : To compensate farmers for damage to agricultural production and farming structures as a result of bad weather (frosts in the provinces of Cosenza and Catanzaro in January/February 2004, and strong winds 2004 in the province of Reggio Calabria on 16 April 2004) 
Legal basis : Legge n. 185/1992 e decreto legislativo n. 102/2004 
Budget : See the approved schemes (C 12/B/95 and NN 54/A/04) 
Aid intensity or amount : Up to 100 % of the cost of the damage 
Duration : Measure applying aid schemes approved by the Commission 
Other details : Measure applying the aid schemes approved by the Commission under State aid C 12/B/95 (Commission Decision 2004/307/EC of 16 December 2003) and NN 54/A/2004 (Commission letter C(2005) 1622 final, dated 7 June 2005) 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption of the decision : 20.2.2006
