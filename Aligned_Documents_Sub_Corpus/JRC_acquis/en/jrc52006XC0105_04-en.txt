Authorisation for State aid pursuant to Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty — Cases where the Commission raises no objections (Text with EEA relevance) 
Authorisation for State aid pursuant to Articles 87 and 88 of the EC Treaty 
Cases where the Commission raises no objections 
(2006/C 2/05) 
(Text with EEA relevance) 
Date of adoption : 6.9.2004 
Member State : Spain 
Aid No : N 336/2004 
Title : Aid in favour of photovoltaic solar energy — Modification of the exiting aid scheme N 460/2001 
Objective : Granting aid to promote the use of photovoltaic solar energy as alternative source for the energy production 
Legal basis : Resolución de la Dirección General del Instituto para la diversificación y ahorro de la energía (IDAE) por la que se regula la concesión de ayudas para el apoyo a la energía solar fotovoltaica 
Budget : EUR 21,6 million per year 
Intensity or amount : 40 % plus regional and SME bonuses 
Duration : Until 31.12.2010 (re-notification after 31.12.2007) 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption : 7.10.2005 
Member State : Germany 
Aid No : N 423/05 
Title : Research for the production of tomorrow (Germany) 
Objective : Prolongation of aid scheme N 353/1999. The aid scheme aims at facilitating research and development projects into new types of production procedures and equipment based on new technologies. The programme is intended to provide support for joint projects, in which companies work together, transcending company barriers and creating synergies. Cooperation with universities and research establishments will also be encouraged (R%amp%D) 
Legal basis : Bundeshaushalt, Kapitel 3006 Titel 683 18 
Budget : EUR 307,76 million 
Intensity or amount : Up to 75 % for industrial research, up to 50 % for precompetitive development activities 
Duration : 1.1.2004 — 31.12.2009 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption : 30.11.2004 
Member State : Spain (Cataluña) 
Aid No : N 459/2004 
Title : Aid to waste minimization in Cataluña — Prolongation of the exiting aid scheme N 154/1999 
Objective : Environmental protection (All sectors) 
Legal basis : Resolución (…), de (…) de 2005, por la que se hace pública la convocatoria de subvenciones para la ejecución de proyectos de minimización de residuos 
Budget : EUR 3 million per year 
Intensity or amount : From 15 % to 40 % depending on the type of measure and the beneficiary 
Duration : From 1.1.2005 to 31.12.2008 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/ 
Date of adoption : 9.9.2005 
Member State : United Kingdom 
Aid No : NN 56/2005 
Title : Low Carbon Research and Development Programme 
Objective : To promote research and development of new innovative low CO2-emission technologies, build confidence in the viability of those technologies, encourage manufacturers to develop commercial low carbon vehicles and acquire new technical knowledge and innovation 
Legal basis : Section 153(1)(y) of the Environmental Protection Act 1990 
Budget : GBP 15 million (EUR 21,8 million) 
Intensity or amount : 75 % for feasibility studies preparatory to industrial research, 50 % for feasibility studies preparatory to pre-competitive development activities, 50 % for industrial research and 25 % for pre-competitive development 
Duration : Three years from the day of this decision 
The authentic text(s) of the decision, from which all confidential information has been removed, can be found at: 
http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgb/state_aids/
